Instructor of the Year:



Every year one student is awarded the "Instructor of the Year". To qualify this student must be instructing in classes and have demonstrated their ability to inspire other students. This student has also shown superior attitude, effort, and spirit. They embody the Tan Ren Do Karate spirit. Congratulations!
