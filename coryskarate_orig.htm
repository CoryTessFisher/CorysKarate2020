Our Goal
 Corys' Karate is dedicated to developing the person as a whole. We firmly believe that learning kicking and punching is a byproduct of a martial arts education. The character development that occurs when learning martial arts is amazing and surpasses the expectations of most of us. Some students have the ability to perform high flying kicks, others outstanding forms, and still others possess great leadership skills. But most importantly, through the practice of martial arts we have the chance to grow in maturity, empathy, and share the important essence of life -- LOVE and working together as a family.

It is amazing what children can achieve when they have self-confidence. Part of developing that self-confidence comes from learning to control themselves, their emotions and from doing things they never thought they could. It's this confidence that learning Tan Ren Do Karate can build in your child.
