Yo-Shin (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° to F)

  1. Look (F), abruptly move right foot to kima stance with simultaneous double outward elbows with open hands palms down, Kiai

  2. Look (R/B), Shuffle right into kima stance with right rear elbow and left square horizontal punch to (B)

  • Look (L/B), Shuffle left into kima stance with left rear elbow and right square horizontal punch to (B)

  1. Look (F), Right outside chop strike, left horizontal punch

  • Left outside chop strike, right horizontal punch

(45° counterclockwise to F/L)

  1. Look (F/L), Slide left foot to left forward stance with a left outward block (fold with left arm on top), right outward block

(90° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), Slide right foot forward to kima stance with left spear hand to diaphragm (palm up), right horizontal punch

  • Slide right foot to left foot, slide right foot out to right forward stance with right outward block, left outward block

(90° counterclockwise to F/L)

  • Look (F/L), Slide left foot forward to kima stance with right spear hand to diaphragm (palm up), left horizontal punch

  1. Right outward block, pivot to a right forward stance (shoulders facing F/R) with left horizontal punch

  • Left outward block, pivot to a left forward stance (facing F/L) with right horizontal punch

(45° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), Slide right foot back to left forward stance with X-block down, X-block up, capture/break with back of hands, left pressing palm heel strike, right reverse punch

  • Right front snap kick, right forward stance with left reverse punch

  1. X-block down, X-block up, capture/break with back of hands, right pressing palm heel strike, left reverse punch

  • Left front snap kick, left forward stance with right reverse punch

  1. Slide right foot forward to execute a right shuto block, slide left foot forward to execute a left shuto block

  • Slide left foot back to execute a right shuto block, slide right foot back to execute a left shuto block

  1. Slide right foot to left neutral with fists together in slow double inverted vertical punch

  • Slide right foot forward to right forward stance with right outward block with left fist reinforcing right elbow, continue motion with left arm to left downward block

  1. Slide left foot to right neutral with hands together in slow double inverted vertical punch

  • Slide left foot forward to left forward stance with left outward block with right fist reinforcing left elbow, continue motion with right arm to right downward block
  1. Grab left, step with right foot to right forward stance with a right horizontal punch, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Slide right foot to left, slide left foot to a left back stance, execute an outside ridgehand strike to temple

(45° clockwise to B/L)

  1. Look (B/L), Slide right foot to left foot and press down with double open hands

  • Fold with double downward fists at waist, step forward with right foot to a deep right forward stance and execute a double sliding punches (snap)

(90° counterclockwise to B/R)

  • Look (B/R), Slide right foot to left foot and press down with double open hands

  • Fold with double downward fists at waist, step forward with left foot to a deep left forward stance and execute a double sliding punches (snap)

(135° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), Slide left foot to right foot, slide right foot to right side kima stance with right outward block, left reverse punch, square up (left on top)

(180° counterclockwise to R)

  • Look (R), slide left foot to right foot with square up (right on top)

  1. Slide left to left side kima stance with left outward block, right reverse punch, square up

(180° clockwise to L)

  • Look (L), slide right foot to left foot with square up (left on top)

(180° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), Slide left foot forward to left forward stance with double knuckle punch

  • Slide left to right, slide right foot forward to right forward stance with double knuckle punch

(90° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Slide right to left, slide right foot to a right side kima stance with right backfist strike, Kiai

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  • Right foot to left knee with simultaneous right inward block w/ left arm horizontal

  • Left foot to right knee with simultaneous right outward block w/ left arm horizontal

  • Left foot to kima stance with right inward hammerfist strike to (F) w/ ready hand

(90° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Fold, abruptly stand up to neutral with simultaneous right slap with back of hand

  • Left inside crescent kick (don’t set down), left side kick, left slap with back of hand in left side kima stance

  1. Right inside crescent kick (don’t set down), right side kick

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  • Look (R), Kima stance with right downward block with left outward block

  • Left downward block with right outward block

(180° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), Slide left foot forward and turn to kima stance with right downward block with left outward block

  • Left downward block with right outward block

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), Slide right foot to left neutral with a right downward hammerfist strike (exaggerated)

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), Slide right foot back to left forward stance with double vertical punches

(180° counterclockwise to R)

  • Slide right foot forward and turn to left forward stance with double vertical punches

(135° counterclockwise to F/L)

  1. Slide right foot to left foot, slide left foot to left shuto block

(90° clockwise to F/R)

  • Slide right foot to left foot, slide right foot to right shuto block, Kiai


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
