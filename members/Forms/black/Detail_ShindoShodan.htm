Shindo Shodan (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° to F)

  • Swing arms around at sides and up to cross in front of body, Scan (L), Scan (R)

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), Slide left foot to a left shuto block

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right reverse punch to stomach, left horizontal punch to face

  1. Right front thrust kick (don’t set down)

(180° clockwise to R)

  • Look (R), Continue motion to set down right foot in right neutral with a simultaneous right outside chop strike

  • Slide right foot to a right forward stance with a right outward block, left horizontal punch, Kiai

(45° counterclockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), “Dive” forward with left foot to a left X-Stance with a low X-Block

  • Slide right foot back to a left forward stance with a slow double outward block

  1. Right front thrust kick

  • Set down in a right side kima stance with a simultaneous right outside chop strike

  1. Grab right, right sliding side kick

(45° counterclockwise to B/L)

  • Continue motion to set down in a left shuto block

  • Right reverse punch (pivot), shift left foot to left side kima stance with a left horizontal punch

(90° clockwise to F/L)

  1. Slide right foot to execute a right shuto block

  • Left reverse punch (pivot), shift right foot to right side kima stance with a right horizontal punch

(45° clockwise to F)

  1. Slide right foot to a right forward stance with a left outward block

  • Left front kick, right rear leg spin kick, right jump-turning inside crescent

  • Set down in right side kima stance with a right front elbow strike, right snapping hammerfist strike to groin, left reverse punch with pivot, right horizontal punch (to ribs), Kiai

(270° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Slide left foot to left shuto block, slide left foot to a left forward stance and grab head to execute a right jumping knee strike (don’t set down)

(180° counterclockwise to L)

  • Continue motion counterclockwise to set down in a left forward stance with a simultaneous right inside ridgehand strike (with left arm horizontal below strike)

  1. Right outside crescent kick, set down in a low kima stance with a simultaneous left sliding punch, right upset finger strike (with left hand palm-up at elbow), left outside chop strike, Kiai

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Slide right foot to left with a right upward block

  • Right knee strike, set down in right forward stance with a right horizontal spearhand strike (left arm horizontal)

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Slide right foot to kima stance with a right inside eye rake strike, chickenbite strike, raking monkey paw strike down chest, right upward chickenhead strike to groin (left hand on right bicep)

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Right back cat stance, right low-high side kick, right sliding side kick, fighting stance

(270° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Left shuto block, slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right reverse punch to chest, left horizontal punch to face

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Right shuto block, slide right foot to a right forward stance with a left reverse punch to chest, right horizontal punch to face

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Slide right foot to left, dramatic fold at left waist

  • Right jump turning outside crescent (set down in left fighting stance)

  1. Right rear leg low/high spin kick, set down right foot

(180° clockwise to B)

  • Continue motion and jump left foot to position of right foot, set right foot back down into right side kima stance and fold with right arm on bottom to execute a slow right pressing outside chop block

  1. Left inside crescent, set down in left side kima stance with a left front elbow strike

(90° clockwise to L)

  • Immediate left downward block and right outward block, right downward block and left outward block, left downward block and right outward block

  1. Low X-Block (right hand on bottom)

  • Grab right at floor (left hand at chest with ridgehand cover), right vertical punch down, Kiai

  1. Snap feet together with a high X-Block

(270° clockwise to B)

  • Slide left foot while swinging crossed arms down by floor and up around to execute a double outward pressing block in a right forward stance

  1. Grab and pull with left hand while stepping forward with left foot, right front thrust kick, right forward stance with simultaneous right horizontal punch

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Shift to a left shuto block

  • Right inside crescent, right jump turning inside crescent, right jump turning inside crescent

  • Set right foot down in a left forward stance, while executing a left outside chop strike, right horizontal punch, Kiai

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
