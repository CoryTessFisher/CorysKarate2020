Unsu (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° to F)

  1. Look (F), Slide left foot to right with a double press down

  • Double press up

  • Double press out

  1. Bring hands around to vertical in front of chest with simultaneous clockwise right knee block (slide foot in a circle), finish move in an elongated right cat stance

  • Right woodpecker finger tip strike

  1. Counterclockwise left knee block (slide foot in circle), finish move in an elongated left cat stance

  • Left woodpecker finger tip strike

  1. Clockwise right knee block (slide foot in circle), finish move in an elongated right cat stance

  • Right woodpecker finger tip strike

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), Set down right foot, slide left foot to a left shotokon stance execute a left outside chop block (fold at right) with right fist at waist

  • Pivot to a left forward stance with a right reverse punch (left fist at waist)

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), Slide right foot to right shotokon stance and execute a right outside chop block (fold at left) with left fist at waist

  • Pivot to a right forward stance with a left reverse punch (right fist at waist)

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), Slide left foot to a left shotokon stance and execute a left outside chop block (fold at right) with right fist at waist

  • Pivot to a left forward stance with a right reverse punch (left fist at waist)

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Slide right foot to right shotokon stance and execute a right outside chop block (fold at left) with left fist at waist

  • Pivot to a right forward stance with a left reverse punch (right fist at waist)

  1. Drop down on forearms and ball of right foot, resting on right hip

  • Left front kick to groin

  1. Flip body (leave hands and elbows/flip hips over) to ball of left foot, resting on left hip

  • Right front kick to groin

(135° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), Set right foot by left foot, stand up in right neutral stance

  • Slide right foot out to right side kima stance with simultaneous double outward press

  1. Slide right foot back to left and fold with left arm on bottom

  • Slide left foot forward to left forward stance and execute a simultaneous left upward ridge hand to nose and right downward palm press at hip

  1. Slide left foot to right and fold with right arm on bottom

  • Slide right foot forward to right forward stance and execute a simultaneous right upward ridge hand to nose and left downward palm press at hip

  • Immediate left horizontal spear hand to throat

  1. Left front thrust kick to head (don’t set down)

(180° clockwise to B)

  • Continue motion by extending right arm back, rotate clockwise to (B), right inward block
  • Set down left leg into right forward stance with simultaneous left reverse punch

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F) (over left shoulder), Slide left foot to left forward stance with simultaneous right horizontal spear hand to throat

  2. Right front thrust kick to head (don’t set down)

(180° clockwise to B)

  • Continue motion by extending left arm back, rotate to counterclockwise to (B), left inward block
  • Set down right leg into left forward stance with simultaneous right reverse punch

  1. Slide left foot to right with an x-block in front of body, continue motion to execute double downward blocks

  • Slide left foot to wide left forward stance while folding with left arm on top

(45° counterclockwise to B/R)

  • Look (B/R), Immediately shift stance direction to a left forward stance facing (B/R) with a simultaneous left downward deflecting block and right outside ridge hand strike

  1. Slide right foot to right side kima stance with a right downward horizontal punch

(180° counterclockwise to F/L)

  • Look (F/L), Quickly shift to left back stance with left downward block

(180° clockwise to B/R)

  • Look (B/R), Quickly shift to right back stance with right downward block

(180° counterclockwise to F/L)

  • Look (F/L), Quickly shift to left shuto block

  1. Slide left foot to left forward stance while bringing right hand around to grab head (pull right hand back by right shoulder and circle down around past hip)

  • Raise right knee, right front thrust kick to knee (with heel of foot) while simultaneously pulling head to right waist, Kiai

  • Set down right foot into right forward stance with a right horizontal punch

(135° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Step with right foot to right side kima stance with a right downward horizontal punch

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (F), Shuffle forward with left foot to left side kima stance with a left ridgehand block

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  • Look (B), Step with left foot to left side kima stance with a left downward chop block

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (F), Shuffle forward with right foot to right side kima stance with a right ridge hand block

  • Left reverse punch, square up

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Shift to a left shuto block

  • Throw right leg up and around to jump 360° and land with hands on ground and left leg extended and right knee bent

  1. Step forward with left to neutral with arms horizontal with left on top and right on bottom, pull arms sideways to dislodge hold

  • Continue motion to slide left foot to left forward stance with a double rib break, press forward

  1. Step forward with right to neutral with arms horizontal with right on top and left on bottom, pull arms sideways to dislodge hold

  • Continue motion to slide right foot to a right forward stance with a double rib break, press forward

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Slide left foot to left forward stance with a left upward block

  • Right horizontal punch, Kiai

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
