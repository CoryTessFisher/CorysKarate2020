Pyung Won (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

Flat hands together and up to chest, flat hands down, step out left with flat hands out at sides

Flat hands horizontal lift to chest, knife hands straight forward at chest

(90° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), fold, slide right foot to right back stance with a right downward deflecting block (Left fist at waist)

(180° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), fold, slide left foot to left back stance for a left outside chop block (right fist at waist)

  • Slide left foot to left forward stance with a simultaneous right upward elbow strike

  1. Right front thrust kick, left turn kick

(180° clockwise to R)

  • Look (R), continue motion to set down in a right back stance with a right shuto block

  1. Circle both hands counterclockwise to execute a right downward deflecting block with a left shuto hand (palm up at chest)

  2. Fold at left waist, step right to right side kima stance with a simultaneous right outward block and left arm horizontal at chest height

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Right foot to kima stance with a right inside hammerfist strike (left arm horizontal), Kiai
  • Immediate left inside hammerfist strike with a starting fold at left shoulder (right arm horizontal)

(90° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), crossover step in front with left foot with arms crossed in front of chest (setup for elbows)
  • Pull to double rear elbows (crossed stance)
  • Step with right foot to right side kima stance with simultaneous double outward blocks (far back)
  1. Right crane stance (knee points to F) with left upward block and right downward block
  • (Don’t set down) Right side kick with hands chambered at left waist
  • Set down in right forward stance with a simultaneous left rising elbow strike
  1. Left front thrust kick, right turn kick

(180° counterclockwise to L)

  • Look (L), continue motion to set down in a left back stance with a left shuto block

  1. Circle both hands counterclockwise to execute a left downward deflecting block with a right shuto hand (palm up at chest)

  2. Fold at right waist, step left to left side kima stance with a simultaneous left outward block and right arm horizontal at chest height

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), reach forward right and bring left foot to right knee
  • Left foot to kima stance with a left inside hammerfist strike (right arm horizontal), Kiai
  • Immediate right inside hammerfist strike with a starting fold at right shoulder (left arm horizontal)

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), crossover step in front with right foot with arms crossed in front of chest (setup for elbows)
  • Pull to double rear elbows (crossed stance)
  • Step with left foot to left side kima stance with simultaneous double outward blocks (far back)
  1. Left crane stance (knee points to F) with right upward block and left downward block
  • (Don’t set down) Left side kick with a simultaneous left outside hammerfist strike and right hand chambered at right waist
  • Set down in left forward stance with a simultaneous right front elbow strike to chest

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), Slide left foot to right foot with flat hands together and up to chest, flat hands down

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
