Sticky Fingers (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Attack:

 Yunbi stance, execute right side first.

Right:

  • Fold at right shoulder, slide right foot forward to a right fighting stance, simultaneously execute a right inside chop strike to neck and a left outside chop block at neck

  • Cup opponent’s hand with right hand, swing opponent’s hands to left side of body, continue motion to execute a right outside ridgehand strike, left fist at waist (movement is in a figure 8 to down by left knee)

  • Right downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body), continue motion to swing right fist around behind body and simultaneously raise right leg, set down into a right fighting stance and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch to stomach

  • Right outside chop strike, swing opponent’s hand clockwise, continue motion to swing right fist around behind body and simultaneously raise right leg, set down into a right fighting stance and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch to stomach

  • Right outside chop strike, right inside chop block, right downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body)

Left:

  • Fold at left shoulder, slide left foot forward to a left fighting stance, simultaneously execute a left inside chop strike to neck and a right outside chop block at neck

  • Cup opponent’s hand with left hand, swing opponent’s hands to right side of body, continue motion to execute a left outside ridgehand strike, right fist at waist (movement is in a figure 8 to down by right knee)

  • Left downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body), continue motion to swing left fist around behind body and simultaneously raise left leg, set down into a left fighting stance and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch to stomach

  • Left outside chop strike, swing opponent’s hand counterclockwise, continue motion to swing left fist around behind body and simultaneously raise left leg, set down into a left fighting stance and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch to stomach

  • Left outside chop strike, left inside chop block, left downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body)


Defense:

Yunbi stance, execute right side first.

Right:

  • Fold at right shoulder, slide left foot back to a right fighting stance, simultaneously execute a left outside chop block and a right inside chop strike

  • Opponent swings hands down to right, continue motion to execute a right outside ridgehand block, left fist at waist (movement is in a figure 8 to down by opponent’s knee)

  • Left sliding punch to stomach, right downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body)

  • Right outside chop block, opponent swings right arm counterclockwise, continue looping motion to execute a right downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body)

  • Left inside chop block, left outside chop strike, right horizontal punch to stomach

Left:

  • Fold at left shoulder, slide right foot back to a left fighting stance, simultaneously execute a right outside chop block and a left inside chop strike

  • Opponent swings hands down to left, continue motion to execute a left outside ridgehand block, right fist at waist (movement is in a figure 8 to down by opponent’s knee)

  • Right sliding punch to stomach, left downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body)

  • Left outside chop block, opponent swings left arm clockwise, continue looping motion to execute a left downward deflecting block (soldier’s block–close to body)

  • Right inside chop block, right outside chop strike, left horizontal punch to stomach
