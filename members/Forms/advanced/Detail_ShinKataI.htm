Shin Kata I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° to F)

  1. Look (F), bring hands together with palms facing body, extend hands straight up above head, bring hands straight down body to waist, bring hands to left waist with right fist on left palm, bring hands to right waist with left palm on right fist, left snapping web hand

  • Left front thrust kick

  • Set down in side left forward stance with a simultaneous right inside eye rake, right snapping backfist strike to side of head

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (L - between B & F), right sliding side kick to (F) while simultaneously executing a downward right vertical punch, left fighting stance, look (B)

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (B), pivot with right front leg sweep to (B) and immediate left shuto block to (F)

  1. Right inside crescent (don’t set foot down), right side kick, left front kick, left forward stance, left horizontal punch, right reverse punch, Kiai

  2. Slide left foot forward to a left side kima stance with a simultaneous left deflecting hammerfist

  • Right falling turn sweep, left fighting stance

  1. Step behind with right foot to an X-Stance with a simultaneously right upset finger strike (grabbing head), pull out to a left fighting stance

  • Step behind with right foot to an X-Stance with a simultaneous downward right vertical punch, pull out to a left fighting stance

  1. Jump right foot forward to a right side kima stance and execute a right upward palm strike

(315° counterclockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), right reverse spin kick, right step-up heel rake, left front elbow strike in right forward stance, Kiai

(45° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), left outside chop, pivot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous right inside chop strike, left hand cover

  • Right front thrust kick, set down right foot crossed in front of left foot, step back with left foot to a right fighting stance

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), shift to a left fighting stance

  2. Right falling turn sweep (down on left knee after)

  • Scoop head with left hand, jump with left to a left forward stance with a simultaneous right palm break to face, Kiai

  1. Step over body with left foot

(180° clockwise to F)

  • Look (F), continue movement to pivot clockwise and execute a right downward front kick to face, Kiai

  • Set right foot behind left into left cat stance, scan


Continue to perform Shin Kata II

. . . or . . .

Birdie out, yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
