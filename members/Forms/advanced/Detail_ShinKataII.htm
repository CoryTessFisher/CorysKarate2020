Shin Kata II (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Continue from the end of Shin Kata I

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), shift to a right back cat stance, right low/high side kick, right side kima stance, right snapping upward hammerfist to groin

  • Slide left foot to right foot (neutral) with a simultaneous right upward elbow strike

(90° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide right foot to a kima stance with a simultaneous right grab, left downward hammerfist, Kiai

(45° clockwise to F/L)

  1. Look (F/L), slide right foot to left with hands crossed, slide right foot back to a deep left pressing shuto block

(90° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), slide left foot to right with hands crossed, slide left foot back to a deep right pressing shuto block

(45° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left with hands crossed in front, bring hands down at sides

  • Right inside crescent kick (don’t set foot down), right side kick

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), continue movement to set down in a left forward stance with a simultaneous shrug to throw hands off

  2. Look (F), right rear elbow strike

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Slide right foot forward and pivot body counterclockwise to execute a left shuto block

(315° counterclockwise to B/L)

  1. Look (B/L), swing right leg up and around to jump 315° counterclockwise and set down to execute a right drop heel rake (going B/L)

  • Slide left foot to right (neutral) with a simultaneous right upward block, slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch

(90° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), slide left foot to right (neutral) with a simultaneous right upward block, slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch

  2. Slide left foot to right with hands together, execute an exaggerated double downward press

  3. Slide right foot to a right side kima stance with a simultaneous right double knuckle punch

  • Left low/high spin kick

(45° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (F), right turning heel rake, Kiai, continue movement to set down in a left fighting stance


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
