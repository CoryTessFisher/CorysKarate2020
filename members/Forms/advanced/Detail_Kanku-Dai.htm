Kanku-Dai (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot, slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), fold and shift stance to execute a right shuto block

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot to right foot, right inside eye rake, left pressing web hand

  • Left front kick, set down in a side left forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal punch

  1. Right outside block, slide right foot back to a side right forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch

  2. Left outside block

(180° clockwise to B)

  • Look (B), slide left foot to right foot and hands at left waist, right low/high side kick

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), set down and execute a left shuto block

  2. Slide right foot forward and execute a right shuto block

  3. Slide left foot forward and execute a left shuto block

  4. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right spear hand strike, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left outside chop strike, right inside chop strike and left hand cover

  2. Right front kick

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (F), set down in left side dragon stance

  1. Low right hand scoop starting in front of right knee, left downward horizontal punch ending in a low left side kima stance

  • Right hand scoop starting in back of left knee, left downward horizontal punch ending in a left side kima stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot in a with hands at right waist, left low/high side kick, set down in a left forward stance with a simultaneous right front elbow strike

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot to left foot with hands at left waist, right low/high side kick, set down in a right forward stance with a simultaneous left front elbow strike

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), pivot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left outside chop strike, right inside chop strike and a left cover hand

  2. Right front kick

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  • Look (B), set down in left side dragon stance

  1. Low right hand scoop starting in front of right knee, left downward horizontal punch ending in a low left side kima stance

  • Right hand scoop starting in back of right knee, left downward horizontal punch ending in a left side kima stance

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot to right foot, fold and slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

(45° counterclockwise to B/R)

  1. Look (B/R), fold and slide right foot out to execute a right shuto block

(135° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), fold and slide right foot out to execute a right shuto block

(45° counterclockwise to B/L)

  1. Look (B/L), fold and slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

(45° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left outside chop strike, right inside chop strike and a left cover hand

  • Right front kick, step behind with left foot to execute a right sliding punch

(180° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), explode forward (step left, step right) to land in an X-stance with a right hammerfist to left palm, Kiai

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), right drop back kick, right sliding side kick, right fighting stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot forward to execute a left shuto block

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal punch to chest, left horizontal punch to face

(180° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot to left foot, slide right foot to execute a right shuto block

  • Slide right foot to a right forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch to chest, right horizontal punch to face

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left foot with hands at left waist, right jump turning outside crescent kick landing in a left side dragon stance

  2. Left fighting stance, right rear leg low/high spin kick, set down right foot

(180° clockwise to B)

  • Look (B), turn clockwise with a hop a bring left foot to position of right foot, right pressing shuto block

  1. Left inside crescent kick, left side kima stance with a left front elbow strike

(90° clockwise to L)

  • Look (L), left downward block with simultaneous right outward block

  • Right downward block with simultaneous left outward block

  • Left downward block with simultaneous right outward block

  1. Downward X-Pressing block in kima stance

  • Snap heels together with a simultaneous upward X-Pressing block

(270° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), Swing arms around and simultaneously slide left foot clockwise to pivot to a right forward stance with a double outward pressing block

  2. Grab and pull with left hand with a step forward with left foot, right front kick, right forward stance with simultaneous right horizontal punch

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), Fold and shift to execute a left shuto block

  • Right inside crescent kick, right jump turning inside crescent, right jump turning inside crescent

  • Continue movement counterclockwise to set down in the splits, extend hands in a press with thumbs and index fingers touching (making a triangular opening), kima stance with right fist to left palm, Kiai


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
