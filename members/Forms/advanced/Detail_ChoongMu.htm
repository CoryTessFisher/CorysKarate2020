Choong Mu (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot, fold and slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

  • Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right inside chop strike and cover with left hand above head

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot to left foot, fold and slide right foot out to execute a right shuto block

  • Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left spear hand strike

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot to right foot, fold and slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), right sliding side kick

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), right running jump side kick, Kiai, right fighting stance

(270° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot to right foot, fold and slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance, grab opponent’s head and jump to execute a right knee strike

(180° counterclockwise to L)

  • Look (L), pivot counterclockwise in air to set down in a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a right inside ridgehand strike and cover with left hand above head

  1. Bring hands to left waist, right rear leg spin kick, left turn kick, left fighting stance

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), pivot to a right fighting stance, left rear leg spin kick

(90° clockwise to B)

  • Look (B), set down with feet together and hands at left waist

  1. Slide right leg forward to a right side kima stance and execute a right double knuckle punch

  2. Grab and jump for 360° counterclockwise for height, fold and execute a right shuto block, Kiai

  3. Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance and execute a right upset finger strike

  • Pull up to a left cat stance with right fist at waist and a left downward deflecting block

  1. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance and execute a right spear hand strike (execute the spear hand strike straight from previous hand position at waist)

(270° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Slide left foot back and pivot counterclockwise to a left forward stance and execute a left twin fist strike (fold at left)

  • Slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance, simultaneously execute a right inside hammerfist block/strike and right backfist strike combination

(180° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), bring hands to left waist, right rear leg side kick to head

  • Bring hands to right waist, left rear leg side kick to head

(180° clockwise to L)

  • Look (L), pivot to set down in a right forward stance and execute a high X-Pressing block

  1. Slide left leg forward to a left forward stance and execute a double rib break, press up

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right leg back to a right forward stance and execute a right upper block, immediately execute a left reverse punch, Kiai


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
