Koryo (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot, fold and slide left foot out to execute a left shuto block

  2. Bring both fists down to left waist, rear leg right low/high side kick, set down into a right forward stance and simultaneously execute a right outside chop strike, left reverse punch

(90° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide right foot to a right cat stance, slide right foot to a kima stance and simultaneously execute a right inside hammerfist block/strike (block a horizontal punch, then strike the jaw)

(270° clockwise to R)

  1. Fold and slide left foot around behind to execute a right shuto block

  2. Bring both fists down to right waist, rear leg left low/high side kick, set down into a left forward stance and simultaneously execute a left outside chop strike, right reverse punch

(90° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot to a left cat stance, left foot to a kima stance and simultaneously execute a left inside hammerfist block/strike (block a horizontal punch, then strike the jaw)

  2. Bring arms to double fold with left arm on top

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Slide left leg back and pivot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left downward deflecting block, right web hand

  1. Right front thrust kick, right forward stance with a simultaneous right downward deflecting block, left web hand

  2. Left front thrust kick, left forward stance with a simultaneous left downward deflecting block, right web hand

  3. Right front thrust kick, right forward stance, simultaneously scoop up opponent’s leg with right hand and execute a left snapping palm to break knee, Kiai

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Slide left foot around and pivot to a right forward stance, execute double outward pressing block (slowly)

  2. Left front thrust kick, left forward stance, simultaneously scoop up opponent’s leg with left hand and execute a right snapping palm to break knee

(90° clockwise to L)

  1. Fold and slide right foot around behind to execute a left shuto block

  2. Grab opponent’s head with left hand, slide right foot behind left foot and simultaneously execute a right vertical punch (punch left palm in front of body at head height), look right/look left, left side kick

(180° clockwise to R)

  • Continue movement to set down in right forward stance, left upset finger strike, pull up to a right cat stance

  1. Step around with left foot to pivot 180° clockwise into a left cat stance, left downward press

  2. Scoop head with left hand, step out with left foot and then pivot 180° counterclockwise, right fist in left palm and step with right foot to a right side horse stance, simultaneously swing arms around to execute a right rising side elbow strike, Kiai

  3. Slide right foot to left foot, fold and slide right foot out to execute a right shuto block

  4. Grab opponent’s head with right hand, slide left foot behind right foot and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch (punch right palm in front of body at head height), right side kick

(180° counterclockwise to L)

  • Continue movement to set down in left forward stance, right upset finger strike, pull up to a left cat stance

  1. Step around with right foot and pivot 180° counterclockwise into a right cat stance, right downward press

  2. Scoop head with right hand, step out with right foot and then pivot 180° counterclockwise, left fist in right palm, and step with left foot to a left side horse stance, simultaneously swing arms around to execute a left rising side elbow strike

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left foot, stand on toes while bringing arms straight up above head and circling down out from sides, palms open

  • Snap right fist to left palm while simultaneously snapping down on heels

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Slide left foot back and pivot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left outside chop strike, left downward deflecting block

  2. Slide right foot to right forward stance with a simultaneous right inward chop strike, right downward deflecting block

  3. Slide left foot to left forward stance with a simultaneous left inward chop strike, left downward deflecting block

  4. Slide right foot to right forward stance with a simultaneous right web hand strike, Kiai
