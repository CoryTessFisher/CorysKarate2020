Ron'in (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° to F)

  1. Look (F), bring closed fists up slowly to cross at chest, slow double downward block

  • Bring open palms up slowly to cross at chest, fast double downward block

  1. Quickly switch left hand to a open palm, simultaneously bring right fist to side

  • Slowly move (straight) left arm up at side, then swing arm around front, then continue movement to bend arm until left palm comes to right shoulder

  • Left outside chop strike, drop down to a kima stance with a right horizontal punch

  1. Double fold with right arm on bottom, right upward block (at an angle), left upward block (right upward block remains up), cross closed fists at chest to fold, double downward block, snap feet together with a simultaneous double ridgehand block

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B) and pivot body to a right side stance with hands at left waist, right side kick

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (F), set down in a left side kima stance with a simultaneous left downward deflecting block and right hand at mid-chest palm up

  1. Raise (straight) left arm up and simultaneously slide left foot to a left forward stance and execute a right vertical punch

  2. Right front thrust kick, right forward stance with a simultaneous left vertical punch

  3. Grab and execute a right knee strike, swing arms around to execute a double downward block in a kima stance, double upward backfist strike

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), bring hands to right waist and execute a left step-up side kick (don’t set down)

(180° clockwise to R)

  • Look (R), cross left foot in front of right to set down, right spin kick, set down in right forward stance with a simultaneous right outward block, left horizontal punch, Kiai

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left and fold with arms at right side, slide left foot forward to a left side kima stance with a simultaneous left downward deflecting block and right hand at mid-chest palm up

  2. Raise (straight) left arm up and simultaneously slide left foot to a left forward stance and execute a right vertical punch

  3. Right front thrust kick, right forward stance with a simultaneous left vertical punch

  4. Bring hands to right waist, high X-Pressing block with closed fists

  • Bring hands to right waist and simultaneously raise right leg, set right foot back in a left forward stance with a high X-Pressing block with open palms (right arm on top)

  • Shift arms around to execute a left outward chop strike (called a “tulip”–bring forearms together, then left palm to right shoulder, then left outward chop strike and right fist to waist), right horizontal punch, Kiai

  1. Right front thrust kick, right forward stance with a simultaneous left vertical punch

  2. Left front thrust kick, left X-Pressing block in a low X-stance

  • Two hand grab and pull to waist, simultaneously slide right foot back to a left forward stance

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide right foot to left and fold at left side, slide right foot out to a right back stance with a simultaneous outside ridgehand strike to knee (big movement) and left hand at mid-chest palm up

  2. Slide left foot to right and fold at right side, slide left foot out to a left back stance with a outside ridgehand strike to knee (big movement) and right hand at mid-chest palm up

(180° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), fold at left side and shift to a right back stance with a simultaneous outside ridgehand strike to knee (big movement) and left hand at mid-chest palm up

  2. Step forward with left foot and grab with left hand, step forward with right foot to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal punch and pull left fist to waist

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide right foot to a kima stance and simultaneously cross closed fists at chest then perform a big circular fold (similar to double downward deflecting blocks)

  • Continue movement to swing arms around for a double downward block

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), pivot and bring left leg up to a left side crane stance with a simultaneous right upward block and left downward block

  • Shift body to face (F) and “square up” left arm with right fist at waist

  • Left outside hammerfist strike with a simultaneous left front thrust kick to chest, set down left foot (left arm remains out), slide right foot forward to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal punch

  1. Pivot and bring right leg up to a right side crane stance with a simultaneous left upward block and right downward block

  • Shift body to face (F) and “square up” right arm with left fist at waist

  • Right outside hammerfist strike with a simultaneous right front thrust kick to chest, set down right foot to a right forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), pivot and bring left leg up to a left side crane stance with a simultaneous right upward block and left downward block

  • Shift body to face (B) and “square up” left arm with right fist at waist

  • Left outside hammerfist strike with a simultaneous left front thrust kick to chest, set down left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal punch

  1. Right front snap kick, set foot back down in previous left forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch

  2. Left front jump kick, set down in a left forward stance to grab and pull with right hand to execute a left horizontal punch, Kiai

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Slide right foot up and bring closed fists to cross at chest

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  • Slide left foot back with a slow double downward block


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
