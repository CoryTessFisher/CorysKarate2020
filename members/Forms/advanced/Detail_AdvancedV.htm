Advanced V (Detailed)
Pyung Ahn Ichi Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), right inside crescent kick, set down in a right forward stance with a simultaneous twin fist strike

  2. Slide right foot forward slightly and simultaneously swing right arm around to execute a right sliding punch

  • Immediately slide left foot forward to a left forward stance and execute a left backfist strike to temple

  1. Look (F), step back with right foot, big step back with left foot (to original position), slide right foot to left and simultaneously bring hands to left side

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Right low pressing side kick to knee, followed by a quick side kick to head

  • Set down and pivot 360° counterclockwise to step out with left foot to a left forward stance with a right inside ridgehand strike and left cover

  1. Right outside crescent kick (slap right hand), set down in horse stance

  • Left sliding punch, right upset finger strike, left outside chop strike, Kiai

(90° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot to neutral with a right upward block

(90° clockwise to B)

  • Pivot clockwise and slide right foot to a right forward stance, right upward knee strike

  • Set down in a low right forward stance with a right upward ridgehand strike to groin

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Slide right foot to a kima stance and execute a right inside eye rake (left hand cover at groin), right chicken bite strike, run right knuckles down opponent’s chest, right upward chicken head strike to groin

(90° clockwise to B)

  1. Continue motion with both hands catching opponent’s arms to throw opponent to the floor and slide right to a right cat stance with open hands crossed in front of chest (X-pressing block), press out

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Pivot to a left cat stance with open hands crossed in front of chest (X-pressing block), press up

  2. Grab and pull left, step forward with right foot to a right side kima stance with a simultaneous right side elbow strike to head

  3. Left reverse punch straight to ribs, right horizontal punch down to ribs

  4. Slide right foot out to a right forward stance, right upward knee strike, set down in a right forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal spear hand strike

  • Right full turn kick, Kiai

  1. Right fighting stance, Look (R), right jump side kick, grab left while airborne and set down on left knee with simultaneous right horizontal punch

  • Grab with left hand past right knee, up to kima stance with a simultaneous right downward chop strike past right knee, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Hop/jump to swap right foot to position of left foot, pivot and set down in a right side kima stance with a right upward palm block to break arm

  • Right reverse spin kick, right sliding side kick, left front kick, right turn kick, Kiai, right fighting stance

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot back to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right upward block and left downward block, left inside hammerfist

  2. Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance with simultaneous right inside chop strike and left cover

  • Right front thrust kick, step behind with left foot with a right twin fist strike

(180° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot out to a left dragon stance (hands start above head and move out)

  2. Slide right foot up to left foot with simultaneous upward X-Pressing block

  • Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance with a simultaneous grab and pull with right hand and left downward hammerfist strike

  1. Slide right foot to left, right turning ax kick

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Step over body to the (L) with left foot and execute a right downward knee strike to back, scan, left downward knee strike to back, scan

  • Stand up, low right front thrust kick to head, Kiai


Birdie out:

Step back with left foot and arms out straight at sides and palms vertical
Step back with right foot and arms tucked in and palms up at shoulders
Slide left foot to right foot and arms down straight at sides and palms down

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
