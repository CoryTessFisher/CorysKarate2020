Bassai (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), bring hands together with palms facing body, extend hands straight up above head, bring hands straight down body

  • Stand on tip-toe, place right fist in left vertical palm, pivot body (still on toes) to (L) and bring arms around to follow movement with right arm horizontal in front of chest

  • Explode forward (swinging right arm around and over head) to land in an X-stance with a right hammerfist to left palm, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), fold with left arm on bottom, slide left leg back to a left forward stance, execute a left outward block, right outward block

(180° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right leg back to a right forward stance, execute a left inward block, right outward block

(90° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), raise right leg and slap with right hand, set down in a right forward stance, execute a right inward block, left outward block

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), fold with left arm on top, slide right foot forward to a kima stance, execute a left outside chop strike

  • Pivot on ball of right foot and execute a right horizontal punch

  1. Double fold with right hand on bottom, right outward block, pivot on ball of left foot and execute a left horizontal punch

  2. Double fold with left hand on bottom, left outward block, pivot on ball of right foot and execute a right horizontal punch

  3. Slide right foot to left, slide right foot forward to execute a right shuto block

  4. Slide left foot forward to execute a left shuto block

  5. Slide right foot forward to execute a right shuto block

  6. Slide right foot back to execute a left shuto block

  7. Shift stance to a left forward stance with an upper X-Pressing block, right side kick

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  • Look (B), pivot counterclockwise and set down with feet together to execute a double inverted vertical punch to temples, Kiai

  1. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance and execute a double rib break, press upward

  • Grab and pull with left hand and simultaneously jump opponent with a big step forward with left foot, right front thrust kick, set down in a right forward stance and execute a right horizontal punch

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot counterclockwise to a left forward stance and execute a right upset finger strike, pull up

  2. Right inside crescent kick, set down to a right side kima stance and simultaneously execute a right hammerfist to groin

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), pressing left outward chop block with right ready hand

  • Right inside crescent, set down in a right side kima stance with a right front elbow strike

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  • Look (R), right downward block with a simultaneous left outward block

  • Left downward block with a simultaneous right outward block

  • Right downward block with a simultaneous left outward block

(90° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide right foot to left and hands to left waist, right inside crescent kick, right side kima stance with a simultaneous double knuckle punch

  2. Slide right foot to left and hands to right waist, left inside crescent kick, left side kima stance with a simultaneous double knuckle punch

  3. Slide left foot to right and hands to left waist, right inside crescent kick, right side kima stance with a simultaneous double knuckle punch

  4. Double hand grab of opponent

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Slide left foot counterclockwise to a kima stance and throw to ground

  • Pivot and bend right knee to execute a right downward backfist to down to head

  • Pivot and bend left knee to execute a left downward backfist to down to groin

  1. Slide left foot to right, slide left foot forward to execute a left shuto block

(45° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Slide right foot forward to execute a right shuto block, Kiai


Slide right foot to left, slide left foot out...
Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
