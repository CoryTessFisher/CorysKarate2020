Kibon III (Detailed)
Kibon San Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left leg back to a right side forward stance, bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with left arm on top

  • Pivot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left downward block

  1. Slide right foot to left foot and simultaneously execute a left upward block, execute a left inverted vertical punch

  • Slide right foot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with right arm on top

  • Bring right leg back and pivot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right downward block

  1. Slide left foot to right foot and simultaneously execute a right upward block, execute a right inverted vertical punch

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot back to right foot and fold to begin a left shuto block (step out to a left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand open at mid chest, palm up)

  1. Fold and slide right forward to begin a right shuto block (finish in right back stance, right outside chop at 45° angle, left hand open at mid chest, palm up)

  1. Fold and slide left foot forward to begin a left shuto block (finish in left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand open at mid chest, palm up)

  2. Grab with left hand and pull to waist, simultaneously step forward into a right forward stance and execute a right horizontal punch, Kiai

(270° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Turn and look (R), bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with left arm on top

  • Bring left leg around back and pivot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left downward block

  1. Slide right foot to left foot and simultaneously execute a left upward block, execute a left inverted vertical punch

  • Slide right foot forward into a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

(180° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with right arm on top

  • Bring right leg back and pivot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right downward block

  1. Bring left foot to right foot and simultaneously execute a right upward block, execute a right inverted vertical punch

  • Slide left foot forward into a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

(90° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot back to right foot and fold to begin a left shuto block (step out to a left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand open at mid chest, palm up)

  1. Fold and slide right foot forward to begin a right shuto block (finish in right back stance, right outside chop at 45° angle, left hand open at mid chest, palm up)

  1. Fold and slide left foot forward to begin a left shuto block (finish in left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand open at mid chest, palm up)

  2. Grab with left hand and pull to waist, simultaneously step forward into a right forward stance and execute a right horizontal punch, Kiai

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with right arm on top

  • Bring right leg back and pivot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right downward block

  1. Slide left foot to right foot and simultaneously execute a right upward block, execute a right inverted vertical punch

  • Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

(270° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Turn to look (L), bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with left arm on top

  • Bring left leg around back and pivot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left downward block

  1. Slide right foot to left foot and simultaneously execute a left upward block, execute a left inverted vertical punch

  • Slide right foot forward into a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with right arm on top

  • Bring right leg back and pivot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right downward block

  1. Slide left foot to right foot and simultaneously execute a right upward block, execute a right inverted vertical punch

  • Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch


Wait...return to yunbi stance when told to do so.
