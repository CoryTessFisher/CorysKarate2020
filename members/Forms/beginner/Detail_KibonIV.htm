Kibon IV (Detailed)
Kibon Ichi Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot, fold to begin left shuto block (step out in left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand mid chest, palm flat up)

  • Slide left foot out to a left forward stance, grab with left hand and pull to waist, simultaneously execute a right punch to groin

  1. Slide right foot to left foot, feet together with knees bent, left horizontal punch to head, right fist returns to waist

  • Pivot to face (F), left side kick to opponent’s chest at (L), set down in left fighting stance

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot to right foot, fold to begin right shuto block (step out in right back stance, right outside chop at 45° angle, left hand mid chest, palm flat up)

  • Slide right foot out to a right forward stance, grab with right hand and pull to waist, simultaneously execute a left punch to groin

  1. Slide left foot to right foot, feet together with knees bent, right horizontal punch to head, left fist returns to waist

  • Pivot to face (F), right side kick to opponent’s chest at (R), set down in right fighting stance

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left foot and bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle parallel to floor) across body with left arm on bottom

  • Slide left leg to a left forward stance, left outward block (right fist to waist), right horizontal punch to chest (left fist to waist)

  1. Right rear leg spin kick, right forward stance, right outward block (left fist to waist), left horizontal punch to chest (right fist to waist)

  2. Left rear leg spin kick, left forward stance, left outward block, (right fist to waist), right horizontal punch to chest, (left fist to waist)

  3. Right rear leg spin kick, right forward stance, right outward block (left fist to waist), left horizontal punch to chest (left fist to waist), Kiai

  4. Bring hands to fold at right waist, strike forward with crossed palms (called a palm strike)

(270° counterclockwise to R)

  • Look (R), fold to begin a left shuto block, bring left foot around behind to turn 270° and complete the left shuto block (finish in a left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand mid chest, palm flat up)

  • Slide left foot out to a left forward stance, grab with left hand and pull to waist, simultaneously execute a right punch to groin

  1. Slide right foot up, feet together with knees bent, left horizontal punch to head, right fist returns to waist

  • Pivot to face (B), left side kick to opponent’s chest at (R), set down in left fighting stance

(180° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot, fold to begin right shuto block (step out in right back stance, right outside chop at 45° angle, left hand mid chest, palm flat up)

  • Slide right foot out to a right forward stance, grab with right hand and pull to waist, simultaneously execute a left punch to groin

  1. Slide left foot to right foot, feet together with knees bent, right horizontal punch to head, left fist returns to waist

  • Pivot to face (B), right side kick to opponent’s chest at (L), set down in right fighting stance

(90° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide right foot to left foot and bring arms to a double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle parallel to floor) across body with left arm on bottom

  • Slide left leg to left forward stance, left outward block (right fist to waist), right horizontal punch to chest (left fist to waist)

  1. Right rear leg spin kick, right forward stance, right outward block (left fist to waist), left horizontal punch to chest (right fist to waist)

  2. Left rear leg spin kick, left forward stance, left outward block (right fist to waist), right horizontal punch to chest (left fist to waist)

  3. Right rear leg spin kick, right forward stance, right outward block (left fist to waist), left horizontal punch to chest (left fist to waist), Kiai

  4. Bring hands to fold at right waist, strike forward with crossed palms (called a palm strike)

(270° counterclockwise to L)

  • Look (L), Fold to begin left shuto block, bring left foot around behind to turn 270° and complete the left shuto block (finish in a left back stance, left outside chop at 45° angle, right hand mid chest, palm flat up)

  • Slide left foot out to a left forward stance, grab with left hand and pull to waist, simultaneously execute a right punch to groin

  1. Slide right foot up, feet together with knees bent, left horizontal punch to head, right fist returns to waist

  • Pivot to face (F), left side kick to opponent’s chest at (L), set down in left fighting stance

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot to right foot, fold to begin right shuto block (step out in right back stance, right outside chop at 45° angle, left hand mid chest, palm flat up)

  • Slide right foot out to a right forward stance, grab with right hand and pull to waist, simultaneously execute a left punch to groin

  1. Slide left foot to right foot, feet together with knees bent, right horizontal punch to head, left fist returns to waist

  • Pivot to face (F), right side kick to opponent’s chest at (R), set down in right fighting stance


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
