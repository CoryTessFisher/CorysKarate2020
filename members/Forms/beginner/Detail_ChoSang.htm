Cho-Sang (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Use flowing movement with small steps. Often performed to the music of God Bless America.

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° clockwise to F)

  1. Slide left foot to right foot and swing arms from sides to above head, cup right fist with left hand, move arms down to a position in front of body, bow

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot forward to execute a left cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a right reverse punch (bring left ridgehand back to guard)

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot to right foot, slide right foot forward to execute a right cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a left reverse punch (bring right ridgehand back to guard)

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left foot, slide left foot forward to execute a left cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a right reverse punch (bring left ridgehand back to guard)

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot to right foot, slide right foot forward to execute a right cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a Left reverse punch (bring right ridgehand back to guard)

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot to a right forward stance facing (R) and execute a left downward deflecting block

  • Continue motion and shift to a left forward stance facing (F) with left cover above head and a right inside chop strike

  • Right rear leg front thrust kick, set down in a right side stance and slide left foot behind right foot, simultaneously execute a right outward block

  1. Step left foot to a left forward stance facing (L) and execute a right downward deflecting block

  • Continue motion and shift to a right forward stance facing (F) with right cover above head and a left inside chop strike

  • Left rear leg front thrust kick, set down in a left side stance and slide right foot behind left foot, simultaneously execute a left outward block

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide right foot forward to execute a right cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a left reverse punch (bring right ridgehand back to guard)

  1. Slide left foot forward to execute a left cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a right reverse punch (bring left ridgehand back to guard)

  1. Slide right foot forward to execute a right cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a left reverse punch (bring right ridgehand back to guard)

  1. Left front thrust kick, set down with feet together

  2. Slide right foot to a deep right forward stance angled to (F/R), simultaneously execute a right ridgehand block and a left downward deflecting block (salute)

  • Slide right foot back to left foot with wrists crossed and palms vertical

  1. Slide left foot to a deep left forward stance angled to (F/L), simultaneously execute a left ridgehand block and a right downward deflecting block (salute)

  • Slide left foot back to right foot with wrists crossed and palms vertical

  1. Extend arms out horizontally at sides of body for double outside chop strikes, bring hands back with wrists crossed and palms vertical

  • Extend arms up at a 45° angle at sides of body for double ridgehand strikes to the temple (double salutes)

  1. Step left foot forward and bend down on right knee, simultaneously extend hands in a press with thumbs and index fingers touching (making a triangular opening)

  • Extend arms out horizontally at sides of body for double outside chop strikes

  1. Slide left foot back to right foot, slide left foot forward to execute a left cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a right reverse punch (bring left ridgehand back to guard)

  1. Slide right foot to left foot with simultaneous high X-block, swing arms from sides to above head, cup right fist with left hand (no bow)

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot back and rotate counterclockwise and bend down on right knee, simultaneously extend hands in a press with thumbs and index fingers touching (making a triangular opening)

  • Extend arms out horizontally at sides of body for double outside chop strikes

  1. Slide left foot back to right foot, slide left foot forward to execute a left cho-tos block

  • Pivot with a right reverse punch (bring left ridgehand back to guard)

  1. Slide right foot to left foot with simultaneous high X-block, swing arms from sides to above head, cup right fist with left hand, bow


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
