Advanced I (Detailed)
Pyung Ahn Ichi Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left leg to right leg, bring arms to double fold with left arm on bottom

  • Slide left leg to a left side kima stance, simultaneously execute a left outward block (blocking a horizontal punch from (L))

  1. Right reverse punch (rotate on ball of right foot), simultaneously bring left fist to waist

(Look 180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), simultaneously slide right leg to left leg and “square up” left arm horizontally at shoulder height in front of body and snap right fist to waist

  2. Bring arms to double fold with right arm on bottom

  • Slide right leg a right side kima stance, simultaneously execute a right outward block (blocking a horizontal punch from (R))

  1. Left reverse punch (rotate on ball of left foot), simultaneously bring right fist to waist

(180° counterclockwise to go L)

  1. Look (L), simultaneously slide left leg to right leg and “square up” right arm horizontally at shoulder height in front of body and snap left fist to waist

(90° clockwise to go F)

  1. Bring right fist down to left fist at waist to fold, step forward with right foot into a right forward stance and simultaneously execute a right twin fist

  2. Pull fists back to sides, step forward with left foot into a left forward stance and simultaneously execute a lower X-Pressing block, pull fists back to sides, execute an upper X-Pressing block with left arm on top

  • Grab with left hand (right fist to waist), step forward with right foot into right forward stance and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch (left fist to waist), Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to face B)

  1. Look (B), turn 180° counterclockwise to face (B), simultaneously execute a right inside crescent kick block (to block a right horizontal punch . . . right arm stays same distance from right leg throughout movement)

  • Set down in a right side horse stance, simultaneously execute a right downward hammerfist to groin

(180° counterclockwise to face F)

  1. Look (F), execute a left outward pressing chop block (slowly)

  2. Right inside crescent kick block (to block a right horizontal punch)

  • Set right foot down into a right side horse stance with simultaneous right front elbow to face, step with left foot behind right foot and execute a right twin fist strike

(180° counterclockwise to face B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot to a left cat stance and simultaneously bring arms up high at right side to fold with hands in fists (right arm straight, left arm bent)

  • Step out with left foot, jump over opponent, and land in an right side X-stance and execute a lower X-Pressing block to (R)

  1. Look (B), slide right leg to a right forward stance and execute a right twin fist strike

(180° counterclockwise to face F)

  1. Look (F), slide left leg back and pivot to a left forward stance, execute a right upset finger strike to the groin

  • Pull up to a left cat stance with right fist by right ear and left fist by left knee

  1. Pivot body counterclockwise to face (L), and bring arms up to shoulder level and execute double backfist strike to (R)

  2. Slide right leg to a right forward stance, execute a left upset finger strike to the groin

  • Pull up to a right cat stance with left fist by left ear and right fist by right knee, Kiai


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
