Yet learning martial arts is as much about mental development as physical fitness, For many people learning martial arts is a journey of self development and realization.

What attributes lead to success in martial arts? Strength? Speed? Memorizing kata? it is none of these things in isolation. To be great at martial arts, to succeed where others fail, to overcome your fears, and to become a stronger person you need passion.

Passion is what drives people to succeed. Total belief in yourself and the task at hand is a part of passion, as is a love for the art that you chose. As you continue through the advanced ranks of Tan Ren Do Karate, use your passion to achieve greatness!
