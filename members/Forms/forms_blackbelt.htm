Forms: Black Belt
A common misbelief is the "a black belt is the ultimate master" stereotype. In reality, a black belt indicates the wearer is competent in a style's basic technique. Since in Tan Ren Do Karate a black belt usually takes anywhere from 3 to 6 years of training to achieve, a good intuitive analogy would be a 1st Dan Black Belt is equivalent to a college Bachelor's degree. The 1st Dan black belt is thus seen not so much as an end, but rather as a beginning, a doorway to advanced learning. The 5th Dan is “Master” in Tan Ren Do Karate and can be viewed similar to a college Master’s degree, and 8th degree black belt “Grandmaster” could be seen being equivalent to a university Doctoral degree.
