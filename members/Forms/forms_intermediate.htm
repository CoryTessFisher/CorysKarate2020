Forms: Intermediate
The concept of belts and rank is probably one that most people associate with martial arts in general, even if they only have a passing interest in the subject. In Tan Ren Do Karate, our belts reflect a member's proven level of competence and (just as importantly) the progression of colors reflects an inner journey that never truly ends. Each belt achieved is truly an accomplishment worthy of respect.

It is also worth noting that achieving a belt isn't just a matter of "spending enough time" in a previous belt. In order to achieve their next rank, a student must demonstrate their proficiency in their current belt's techniques. As you practice these forms make the effort to improve yourself and progress to that next level. Focus upon the basics and become just that little bit better every time.
