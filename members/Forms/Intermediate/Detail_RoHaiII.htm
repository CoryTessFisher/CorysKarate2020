Ro-Hai II (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot to right foot, slide left foot forward to execute a left shuto block

... or ...

Continue from RoHai I

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a right monkey paw to throat, bring left fist to waist
  1. Rotate right wrist to grab and pull opponent, simultaneously execute a right rear leg side kick to head

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  • Set down into a horse stance and execute a downward X-pressing block

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Slide right foot back and pivot into a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right upward block, bring left fist to waist
  • Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance and execute a left horizontal punch, Kiai

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Slide right foot back and pivot into a right side horse stance, simultaneously bring arms to double fold with right arm on bottom

  • Execute a right outward pressing chop block (slowly)

  1. Left reverse punch rotating on ball of left foot, simultaneously bring right fist to waist

  • Square up” left arm horizontally at chest height and return to a right side horse stance

  1. Slide right foot back and pivot to a right forward stance, grab head (hair) with hands in fists and execute a left knee strike
  • Set down in a low left X stance and execute a low X-Pressing block

  1. Slide right foot back to a left forward stance and execute a double outward pressing block (slowly)

  2. Start Kiai...slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance and execute a right front elbow strike

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Continue movement and pivot to a left cat stance and simultaneously execute a left downward deflecting block (right fist to waist) ...End Kiai
  1. Slide left foot back to right cat stance with a simultaneous right outside ridge hand strike (left fist to waist)
  • Step back with left foot (fold with both hands), slide right foot back to a right cat stance with a simultaneous right inward chop strike
  • Step back with left foot (fold with both hands), slide right foot back to a right cat stance with a simultaneous right inward chop strike
  1. Slide right foot back to a left cat stance with a simultaneous left outside ridge hand strike (right fist to waist)
  • Step back with right foot (fold with both hands), slide left foot back to a left cat stance with a simultaneous left inward chop strike
  • Step back with right foot (fold with both hands), slide left foot back to a left cat stance with a simultaneous left inward chop strike

(360° clockwise to F)

  1. Raise left leg and slap with left hand, spin 360° clockwise to right forward stance and simultaneously execute right backfist to side of opponent’s head

  2. Slide left pressing web hand down underside of right arm to opponent’s throat

  • Rear leg left front kick to location of left hand, set down into a left forward stance with a simultaneous right reverse punch

  1. Right jump front kick, left forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch, right reverse punch, Kiai

 

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
