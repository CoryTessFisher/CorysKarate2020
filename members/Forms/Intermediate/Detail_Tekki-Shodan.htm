Tekki-Shodan (Detailed)
Iron Horse

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 
This form is executed along a straight line–to the left and right.

Execute entire form below twice. The first time is performed slowly and the second time is performed faster.

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(Facing (F))

  1. Left foot to neutral, bring hands together with palms facing body, extend hands straight up above head, bring hands straight down body to waist

(Movement and strikes (L), Body facing (F))

  • Continue movement to extend left hand out to press and bring right hand palm up below chest with a simultaneous big step with right foot over left foot
  • Step out with left foot to a horse stance and pivot on ball of right foot to execute a right front elbow strike
  1. Pivot back to a horse stance and simultaneously drop hands to left waist

(Movement and strikes (R), Body facing (F))

  • Right downward block

  • Pivot on ball of left foot to execute a left reverse punch, square it up

(Facing (F))

  1. Big step with left foot over right, simultaneously execute a left backfist to nose

  • Grab opponent’s ear with right hand and pull right, step out with right foot to a horse stance and execute a right deflecting hammerfist with left arm horizontal and fist at right elbow

  1. Bring left foot to right knee and simultaneously execute a right outward block (with left fist remaining at right elbow)

  • Bring right foot to left knee and simultaneously execute a right inward block (with left fist remaining at right elbow)

(Movement and strikes (R), Body facing (F))

  • Set down right foot into a horse stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch with left arm ‘squared up’ in front of body

  • Cross hands in front of body and continue movement to extend right hand out to press and bring left hand palm up below chest

  • Pivot on ball of left foot to execute a left front elbow strike

  1. Pivot back to a horse stance and simultaneously drop hands to right waist

(Movement and strikes (L), Body facing (F))

  • Left downward block

  • Pivot on ball of right foot to execute a right reverse punch, square it up

(Facing (F))

  1. Big step with right foot over left, simultaneously execute a right backfist to nose

  • Grab opponent’s ear with left hand and pull left, step out with left foot to a horse stance and execute a left deflecting hammerfist with right arm horizontal and fist at left elbow

  1. Bring right foot to left knee and simultaneously execute a left outward block (with right fist remaining at left elbow)

  • Bring left foot to right knee and simultaneously execute a left inward block (with right fist remaining at left elbow)

(Movement and strikes (R), Body facing (F))

  • Set down right foot into a horse stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch with right arm ‘squared up’ in front of body

(Facing (F))

  1. Slide left foot to right, slide right foot out to yunbi stance

After executing the entire form twice...Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
