Tekki-Shodan (Quick)

Tekki-Shodan
Iron Horse

THIS FORM IS PERFORMED TWICE BACK TO BACK THE FIRST TIME SLOWLY AND THE SECOND TIME AS QUICKLY AS YOU CAN WHILE MAINTAINING GOOD TECHNIQUE (3 TIMES AS FAST). THIS FORM SHOULD BE VERY HARD CRISP AND POWERFUL.

Yunbi, Chayuit, Kiyunuit, Yunbi
Left foot to neutral, Bring palms together, Press up slowly, Press down slowly
Look left, Press left hand out, Big step with right, Step left to kima stance
Pivot, Right elbow strike, Kima stance (chamber), Look right, Right downward block
Pivot, Left reverse punch, Square it up
Look front, Big step left, Left backfist to nose
Grab ear with right hand and pull back
Step right to kima stance, Right inside hammerfist
Left crane stance, Right reinforced outer block
Right crane stance, Right reinforced inward block
Look right, Kima stance, Right horizontal punch (with left arm “squared up’)

Press right hand out to right side,
Pivot, Left elbow strike, Kima stance (chamber), Look left, Left downward block
Pivot, Right reverse punch, Square it up
Look front, Big step right, Right backfist to nose
Grab ear with left hand and pull back
Step left to kima stance, Left inside hammerfist
Right crane stance, Left reinforced outer block
Left crane stance, Left reinforced inward block
Look left, Kima stance, Left horizontal punch (with right arm “squared up’)
Slide left foot to right, Yunbi

REPEAT beginning by sliding left foot to neutral,
Bringing palms together, and pressing up and performing the form quickly a second time

To finish from last Yunbi, Chayuit, Kiyunuit, Yunbi
