Ro-Hai I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(0° to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right foot to left foot, simultaneously fold with arms at left side

  • Slide right foot to kima stance with a simultaneous right downward deflecting block with left hand mid-chest, palm flat up

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to right foot, slide left out out and fold to execute a left shuto block

  • Right reverse punch rotating on ball of right foot, simultaneously bring left fist to waist, “square up” right arm horizontally at chest height and return to kima stance

(135° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Slide left foot to right foot in left neutral stance and fold with arms up and at right side

  • Step forward with left foot to a downward left X-pressing block in a low X-stance

  1. Step back with left foot to a right crane stance, simultaneously execute right downward deflecting block with left palm open above head (use for balance)

  • Set down right foot to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right outward chop

  1. Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch, right horizontal punch

(90° counterclockwise to F/L)

  1. Step back with left foot to a right crane stance, simultaneously execute right downward deflecting block with left palm open above head (use for balance)

  • Set down right foot to right forward stance with a simultaneous right outward chop

  1. Slide left foot to left forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch, right horizontal punch

(45° clockwise to F)

  1. Right sweep to a low kima stance, simultaneously swing right fist around counterclockwise in a big circle to punch straight down between legs (right elbow and right knee should pass in the air), Kiai

  2. Step back with left foot to a right crane stance, simultaneously execute a right downward deflecting block with left palm open above head (use for balance)

  • Set down right foot to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right outward chop

  1. Left horizontal punch, right horizontal punch (no step)

  2. Slide right foot back to left foot and snap feet together, simultaneously snap both fists to left waist

  • Slide right foot forward to right side kima stance with a simultaneous right double knuckle punch

  1. Slide right foot back to left foot and snap feet together, simultaneously snap both fists to right waist

  • Slide left foot forward to left side kima stance with a simultaneous left double knuckle punch

  1. Slide left foot back to right foot and snap feet together, simultaneously snap both fists to left waist

  • Right inside crescent, set down in right side horse stance with a simultaneous right double knuckle punch

(360° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Grab opponent’s staff with both hands and throw to (L), jump back 360° counterclockwise for distance

  • Set down and fold to execute a right shuto block, Kiai

  1. Step back with right foot and fold to execute a left shuto block

  2. Bring fists to left waist, rear leg right low/high spin kick

  • Left turn kick, Kiai, set down with a left shuto block
