Advanced IV (Detailed)
Pyung Ahn Shi Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide right foot to left foot and right hand to left shoulder, left fist at waist

  • Slide right foot back to left side kima stance, simultaneously pull opponent’s hand up off left shoulder and execute a left sliding punch

  1. Slide left foot to pivot into a left forward stance, simultaneously rotate right hand clockwise in front of body (to force opponent to bend forward), left fist at waist

  • Right front kick to ribs, right forward stance

  1. Left inside chop strike to back of neck, jump and right outside ridgehand strike (scoop under opponent) to front of neck

  • Set down in right forward stance and simultaneously execute a left downward hammerfist to spine

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot to left foot and left hand to right shoulder, right fist at waist

  • Slide left foot back to right side kima stance, simultaneously pull opponent’s hand up off right shoulder and execute a right sliding punch

  1. Slide right foot to pivot into a right forward stance, simultaneously rotate left hand counterclockwise in front of body (to force opponent to bend forward), left fist at waist

  • Left front kick to ribs, left forward stance

  1. Right inside chop strike to back of neck, jump and left outside ridgehand strike (scoop under opponent) to front of neck

  • Set down in left forward stance and simultaneously execute a right downward hammerfist to spine

(270° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), right drop back kick, right sliding side kick, left front thrust kick, right turning heel rake kick, Kiai

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot to a left cat stance and fold with arms high at right shoulder

  • Step forward with left foot into a left forward stance and simultaneously execute a low X-Pressing block (hands closed), execute a high X-Pressing block (hands open)

  1. Grab and pull opponent’s left arm up high with left hand, step forward with right foot to a right side kima stance and simultaneously press opponent’s left shoulder down with right hand

  2. “Check” leg with right foot, right upward kick to chest

  • Right downward elbow to spine (sandwich opponent between knee and elbow), Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), right inside crescent kick swinging arms around & releasing opponent, continue motion to bring right foot to left knee and hands to left side (do not set foot down)
  • Right eye rake, left eye rake, set foot down in a right side kima stance and simultaneously execute a right chicken head strike to head, Kiai

(45° counterclockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), right crane stance, right side kick, set right foot down crossed in front of left foot

(45° counterclockwise to F)

  • Continue motion to step back with left foot, pivot 360° counterclockwise (jumping for distance), set down and fold to execute a right shuto block, Kiai

  1. Slide right foot back to a left X-stance facing (F/R), scan 90° for new opponents (hands crossed with palms vertical)

  • Slide left foot back to a right X-stance facing (F/L), scan 90° for new opponents (hands crossed with palms vertical)

  • Slide right foot back to a left cat stance facing (F), scan 180° for new opponents (hands crossed with palms vertical)


Birdie out: Step back with left foot and arms out straight at sides and palms vertical
Step back with right foot and arms tucked in and palms up at shoulders
Slide left foot to right foot and arms down straight at sides and palms down

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
