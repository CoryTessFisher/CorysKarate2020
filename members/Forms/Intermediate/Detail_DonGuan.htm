Don Guan (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

((F))

  1. Look (F), slap both legs, bring right foot up to left knee with hands crossed (in X-Block) and palms vertical, spin hands around each other and set right foot down to a left fighting stance

  • Left grab, slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

  1. Left reverse punch, right horizontal punch

  2. Slap left leg with back of left hand and finish with left chop blocking sun, slide left foot to position of right foot, right side crane stance with a simultaneous right upward block and left downward block

  • Position left hand at front of waist with palm up, slap right leg with back of right hand, right chicken head strike to left hand, set down right foot into a right side kima stance and simultaneously execute a right backfist to temple

  1. Slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

  2. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance, pivot to a right diagonal kima stance while executing a simultaneous left upward block and a right vertical punch (at waist height)

  • Pivot for a left forearm strike, right forearm strike, hands up (feet remain in a right diagonal kima stance)

(Facing (F), Movement (B))

  1. Slide right foot back to a left back stance, simultaneously execute a left hand chop block

  2. Slide left foot back to a right back stance, simultaneously execute a right hand chop block

  3. Slide right foot back to a left back stance, simultaneously execute a left hand chop block

((F))

  1. Left inside crane block, slide right foot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

  2. Slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

  3. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance, pivot to a right diagonal kima stance while executing a simultaneous left upward block and a right vertical punch (at waist height)

  • Pivot for a left forearm strike, right forearm strike, hands up (feet remain in a right diagonal kima stance)

(Facing (F), Movement (B))

  1. Bring arms to double fold with right arm on bottom

  • Slide right foot back to a left forward stance (fold), slide left foot back to a right forward stance and execute a right upward block

  • Immediately slide right foot back to a left forward stance and execute a left downward block

((F))

  1. Left inside crane block, slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

  2. Cross vertical open palms in front of body and swing hands down to slap legs and press out horizontally at shoulder height

  3. Grab and pull with right hand, slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

  4. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance, pivot to a right diagonal kima stance while executing a simultaneous left upward block and a right vertical punch (at waist height)

  • Pivot for a left forearm strike, right forearm strike, hands up (feet remain in a right diagonal kima stance)

((F))

  1. Position left hand at front of waist with palm up, slide left foot to position of right foot, right side crane stance, slap right leg with right hand, right chicken head strike to left hand

  • Jump clockwise for height to a left side cat stance, execute a simultaneous right upward block and a left downward block

(Facing (B), Movement (B))

  1. Look (B), shift to right cat stance and position left hand at front of waist with palm up, right side crane stance, slap right leg with right hand, right chicken head strike to left hand

  • Jump clockwise for height to a left side cat stance, execute a simultaneous right upward block and a left downward block

  1. Start a right inside crane block

  • Slide left foot to a deep left forward stance, simultaneously finish right inside crane block by pulling right hand behind back and execute a left horizontal punch

  1. Slide left foot back to a left cat stance with hands crossed and palms vertical

  • Pivot to a left side cat stance (with hands crossed and palms vertical)
