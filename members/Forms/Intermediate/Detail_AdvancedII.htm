Advanced II (Detailed)
Pyung Ahn Ni Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left leg to right leg and fold at right side, slide left foot to a left back stance and simultaneously execute a left outside chop block and a right upward block (with palm open)

  2. Execute a right sliding punch to throat (pivot body with right heel off ground)

  • Pivot abruptly back into a left side horse stance (move left foot slightly), simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch to body

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right leg to left leg and fold at left side, slide right foot to a right back stance and simultaneously execute a right outside chop block and a left upward block (with palm open)

  2. Execute a left sliding punch to throat (pivot body with left heel off ground)

  • Pivot abruptly back into a right side horse stance (move right foot slightly), simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch to body

(90° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), simultaneously slide right foot to left foot and fists to left waist, right low/high side kick

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), set down in a left back stance and fold to execute a left shuto block

  2. Slide right foot forward and fold to execute a right shuto block

  3. Slide left foot forward and fold to execute a left shuto block

  4. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance, simultaneously swing arm around to execute a right vertical spear hand strike to diaphram, Kiai

  5. Grab head with left hand, slide right foot forward to a right side horse stance and simultaneously execute a right front elbow strike

(225° counterclockwise to B/L)

  1. Look (B/L), slide left foot to right foot, slide left foot forward and fold to execute a left shuto block

  • Grab sleeve, pull to a left cat stance with right reverse punch to chest, set down left foot into a left side horse stance and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

  1. Slide right foot forward and fold to execute a right shuto block

  • Grab sleeve, pull to a right cat stance with left reverse punch to chest, set down right foot into a right side horse stance and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

(90° counterclockwise to B/R)

  1. Look (B/R), slide right foot forward and fold to execute a right shuto block

  • Grab sleeve, pull to a right cat stance with left reverse punch to chest, set down right foot into a right side horse stance and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

  1. Slide left foot forward and fold to execute a left shuto block

  • Grab sleeve, pull to a left cat stance with right reverse punch to chest, set down left foot into a left side horse stance and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

(45° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), bring arms to a double fold with right arm on bottom

  • Slide left foot back to deep left forward stance, simultaneously execute a right outward pressing block

  • Right front thrust kick, deep right forward stance with a simultaneous left horizontal punch

  1. Bring arms to a double fold with left arm on bottom, left outward pressing block

  • Left front thrust kick, left forward stance with a simultaneous right horizontal punch

  1. Slide right foot to left foot with fists to left waist, slide right foot to a right forward stance with a simultaneous right twin fist strike

(45° counterclockwise to B/R)

  1. Look (B/R), slide right foot to left foot and bring fists to left waist, right back kick

(180° counterclockwise to F/L)

  • Set down in a left back stance and fold to execute a left shuto block

  1. Grab and pull head with left hand and execute a right vertical punch to head (pivot body with right heel off ground)

(90° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Slide right foot forward and fold to execute a right shuto block

  • Grab and pull head with right hand and execute a left vertical punch to head(pivot body with left heel off ground), Kiai


Slide right foot to left foot, slide left foot out...

Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
