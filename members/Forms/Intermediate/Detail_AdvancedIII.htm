Advanced III (Detailed)
Pyung Ahn San Don

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), bring arms to double fold with left arm on bottom, right grab at shoulder

  • Slide left leg back to a side right forward stance (with body still facing (F)) and execute a left inward block

  • Pivot to a left forward stance and execute a right ridgehand strike to the groin

  1. Slide right foot to left foot, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch to the sternum

  • Slide right foot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch to the face

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot to left foot and bring arms to a double fold with right arm on bottom, left grab at shoulder

  • Slide right foot back to a side left forward stance (with body facing (F)) and execute a right inward block

  • Pivot to a right forward stance and execute a left ridgehand strike to the groin

  1. Slide left foot to right foot, simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch to the sternum

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch to the face

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F) shift arms to a fighting stance (feet remain in a left forward stance)

  2. Right turn kick, right fighting stance

  3. Slide right foot to right forward stance and execute a double palm press

  • Right jump front kick, right step side kick, Kiai

(135° counterclockwise to B/R)

  1. Look (B/R), bring arms to fold for a scissor block with left arm on top (cover left)

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance, simultaneously execute a left scissor block (left downward deflecting block and a right ridgehand block), left backfist to head

  1. Slide right foot to left foot and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch, slide left foot to a left side kima stance and simultaneously execute a left front elbow strike

(90° clockwise to B/L)

  1. Look (B/L), slide left foot to right foot and bring arms to fold for a scissor block with right arm on top (cover right)

  • Slide right foot to a right forward stance, simultaneously execute a right scissor block (right downward deflecting block and a left ridgehand block), right backfist to head

  1. Slide left foot to right foot and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch, slide right foot to a right side kima stance and simultaneously execute a right front elbow strike

(45° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide right leg to neutral and simultaneously bring arms to double fold (with arms parallel to each other at a 45° angle to floor) across body with left arm on bottom, left outward block, right horizontal punch

  • Slide left leg to a left forward stance while executing a left inside chop strike

  1. Right outward block

  • Slide right leg to left leg and simultaneously execute a left horizontal punch

  • Slide right leg to a right forward stance while executing a right inside chop strike

  1. Left outward block

  • Slide left leg to right leg and simultaneously execute a right horizontal punch

  • Slide left leg to a left forward stance while executing a left inside chop strike

  1. Turn left hand to vertical (cup opponent’s head) and press down at waist with right hand (fold)

  2. Start Kiai...lift right foot to a right crane stance and simultaneously execute a right chicken head strike to head

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Set down right foot and continue movement to slide left foot out to a kima stance, simultaneously look over left shoulder (B) and execute a left back elbow (cup right hand at left shoulder to catch opponent's head)...End Kiai

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Shift to right back cat stance and look over right shoulder (B), right back kick

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  • Set down in a left cat stance, scan 180° for new opponents (hands crossed with palms vertical)


Birdie out:

Step back with left foot and arms out straight at sides and palms vertical
Step back with right foot and arms tucked in and palms up at shoulders
Slide left foot to right foot and arms down straight at sides and palms down


Yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, and yunbi stance.
