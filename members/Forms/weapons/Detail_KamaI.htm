Kama I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance

Look L, look R, look L and flip left kama to 2nd position...

Ready hands!!!!!

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), dramatic set down into a side kima stance with a left punch

  • Pivot, right 7 strike

  • Side kima stance, left punch

  • Pivot, right punch

  • Side kima stance, left rake

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R)

  • Pivot, left punch

  • Side kima stance, right 7 strike

  • Pivot, left punch

  • Side kima stance, right punch

  • Pivot, left rake

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F)

  • Slide right foot to left, flip left kama to 1st position, right foot back to left forward stance with a left downward block

  1. Left foot back to a right forward stance with a right upward block

  2. Step with left to a left forward stance with a double right downward diagonal strike

  3. Step with right to a right forward stance with a double left downward diagonal strike

  4. Step with left to a left forward stance with a double right downward diagonal strike

  • Continue motion to spin clockwise (on left foot), set down in a right forward stance with a double right downward diagonal strike, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), shift to a fighting stance

  2. Step with right to a right forward stance with a right 7 strike

  3. Step with left to a left forward stance with a left 7 strike

  4. Step with right to a right forward stance with a right 7 strike

  5. Flip left kama to 2nd position, step left to a side kima stance with a left rake, continue motion to spin clockwise (on left foot), set down in a right forward stance with a right outside strike, Kiai


Flip left kama to 1st position and spin counterclockwise with left foot to yunbi stance, chayuit, kiyunyuit, return to yunbi stance
