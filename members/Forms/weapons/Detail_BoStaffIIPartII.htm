Bo-Staff III (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Walk forward with staff behind right arm, swing staff clockwise to a vertical position in front of body, bow, slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance facing (F) and position staff under left shoulder (position staff as if after executing a stab to opponent’s head at (F))

. . . or . . .

Continue from end of Bo-Staff II

(0° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide right hand to hold end of staff, using right hand swing staff above head and to the right in a fast 360° arc, simultaneously slide right foot back to a left side dragon stance

  • Complete movement with right leg bent and staff held above head at the angle of straight left leg and resting on back of left hand

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), pivot body clockwise and set down on bent left knee, low horizontal staff block (blocking a kick), high horizontal staff block (blocking an overhead strike)

  2. Right jump front kick, bring staff to vertical at right side, left low/high spin kick, right turn kick

  • Set down into a right side kima stance and simultaneously stab to opponent’s face, Kiai

(270° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), turn right hand over on staff, slide right foot counterclockwise to a left forward stance and simultaneously execute a vertical staff block (blocking a spin kick)

  2. Slide right foot forward to a right forward stance and simultaneously swing staff back around right side to end under left shoulder with a strike vertically down to opponent’s head on ground

  3. Scoop staff and swing up clockwise (to dislodge opponent off staff) to a horizontal position and simultaneously turning right hand over on staff

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), right drop kick (don’t let staff touch ground), right sliding side kick,

  • Set down in right side fighting stance and slide left foot to a left forward stance and simultaneously swing staff back around right side to end under left shoulder with a strike vertically down to opponent’s head on ground

  • Stab to opponent’s head on ground

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot to right foot, slide left foot forward to a left forward stance and simultaneously execute a helicopter strike to under left shoulder

  2. Execute a helicopter strike to under right shoulder

  3. Slide right foot forward to a right kima stance and simultaneously swing staff around to stab to opponent’s face (swing staff to a vertical position and then stab), Kiai


Slide right foot back to left foot and swing staff counterclockwise to behind right arm, bow.
