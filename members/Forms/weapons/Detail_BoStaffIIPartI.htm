Bo-Staff II (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Walk forward with staff behind right arm, swing staff clockwise to a vertical position in front of body, bow, position staff on top of right shoulder with feet together and facing (F/R)

. . . or . . .

Continue from end of Bo-Staff 1

(135° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide left foot forward to a left forward stance, simultaneously swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  2. Slide left foot back to right foot and bring staff to vertical at right side, left front thrust kick, set down with feet together

  3. Bring staff to vertical at left side, right low/high side kick, set down in a right side kima stance and simultaneously stab to opponent’s stomach, Kiai

(135° counterclockwise to B/R)

  1. Pivot body and staff slightly to look (B/R)

  • Bring staff to vertical at left side, right low/high spin kick, left turn kick

(180° counterclockwise to F/L)

  • Set down in a right forward stance and simultaneously execute a horizontal staff block above head against a strike from behind & above

(135° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), slide left foot back to a left forward stance and execute a helicopter strike to under left shoulder

  2. Execute a helicopter strike to under right shoulder

  3. Slide left foot back to right foot and simultaneously execute a helicopter strike to under left shoulder

  4. Swing staff up vertically to top of right shoulder to fold

  • Slide left foot back to a right forward stance and simultaneously swing staff down vertically to under right shoulder and strike opponent’s head on ground

  1. Slide right foot to left foot, slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance and simultaneously swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Pivot body and staff slightly to look (F)

  • Slide right foot forward to a right side kima stance and simultaneously swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  1. Slide left foot forward to a left side kima stance and simultaneously swing staff right horizontally to under right shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  2. Slide right foot around behind and pivot clockwise to a right side kima stance and simultaneously stab to opponent’s face, Kiai

Continue to perform Bo-Staff III

. . . or . . .

Slide right foot back to left foot and swing staff counterclockwise to behind right arm, bow.
