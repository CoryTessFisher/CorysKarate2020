Bo-Staff I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Walk forward with staff behind right arm, swing staff clockwise to a vertical position in front of body, bow, bring staff to a vertical position in front of right arm.

(0° to F)

  1. Look (F), bring straight left arm to a horizontal position out at left side with open palm down, swing arm around to in front of body, bend elbow at last moment to bring hand to hold staff

  2. Slide left foot forward to a left forward stance, simultaneously bring top of staff down at a 45° angle (same angle as right leg)

  • Swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  1. Swing staff right horizontally to under right shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  2. Swing staff up vertically to top of right shoulder and strike up opponent’s groin

  3. Swing staff down vertically to under right shoulder and strike top of opponent

  4. Slide left foot to right foot to a left side stance and fold with staff high at right shoulder

  • Slide left foot forward to a left side kima stance and stab down on opponent’s head, Kiai

(180° clockwise to B)

  1. Pivot body and staff slightly to look (B), slide left foot forward to a left forward stance and simultaneously swing staff right horizontally to under right shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  2. Swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

(90° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (R), pivot to a right forward stance, simultaneously swing staff right horizontally to under right shoulder and strike opponent’s chest

  2. Swing staff up vertically to top of right shoulder and strike up opponent’s groin

  3. Swing staff down vertically to under right shoulder and strike top of opponent

  4. Swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  5. Look (F), slide right foot back to left foot, simultaneously swing staff down to sweep opponent’s leg

  • Slide left foot forward to a side left forward stance (still looking (F)), simultaneously swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  1. Rotate staff counterclockwise to trap and rotate opponent’s arm, stab opponent’s face

(90° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), pivot to a right forward stance, simultaneously swing staff right horizontally to under right shoulder and strike across opponent’s head

  2. Swing staff up vertically to top of right shoulder and strike up opponent’s groin

  3. Swing staff left horizontally to under left shoulder and strike across opponent’s head, Kiai

(45° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Slide right foot back to right cat stance, simultaneously swing staff counterclockwise to under right arm

  • Right side kick, swing staff clockwise to right side of body and set down in a left fighting stance

  1. Slide left foot back to right foot and fold with staff high at right shoulder

  • Slide left foot forward, slide right foot to left foot and swing staff up vertically to top of right shoulder and strike up opponent’s groin, Kiai


Continue to perform Bo-Staff II

Slide right foot back to left foot and swing staff counterclockwise to behind right arm, bow.
