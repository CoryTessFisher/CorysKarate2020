Sai Part I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start in yunbi stance, slide right foot out and flip sais out to 2nd position, scan, return to 1st position and slide right foot in to yunbi stance, bow

(45° clockwise to F/R)

  1. Look (F/R), slide left foot forward to a right reverse bo stance, with a right downward block and a left upward block

  • Fold with right sai, rotate to a right forward stance with a right backfist strike, left reverse punch

(45° counterclockwise to F)

  • Look (F), shift to a kima stance with a right horizontal punch

(45° counterclockwise to F/L)

  1. Look (F/L), slide right foot forward to a left reverse bo stance, with a left downward block and a right upward block

  • Fold with left sai, rotate to a left forward stance with a left backfist strike, right reverse punch

(45° clockwise to F)

  • Look (F), shift to a kima stance with a left horizontal punch

  1. Double cross fold, right crane stance with a double downward strike (to groin)

  • Set down in a right kima stance with a right inside elbow strike, right outside elbow strike, right backfist strike (vertical), flip back

  1. Spin counterclockwise (step right, step left), left kima stance with a left outside elbow strike
  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right reverse punch
  • Sai vertical, right front thrust kick, left reverse punch, right jump front kick, right forward stance with a right horizontal punch, left reverse punch, Kiai

(270° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide left foot back to a left forward stance with a left backfist strike
  • Step right to a right forward stance with a right reverse punch, shuffle forward with a right overhead inside chop strike

(180° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide right foot back to a right forward stance with a right backfist strike
  • Step left to a left forward stance with a left reverse punch, shuffle forward with a left overhead inside chop strike

(90° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), step left foot back to a left forward stance with a left downward block, left backfist strike, left inside chop strike, left downward block

  • Step right to a right forward stance with a right horizontal punch, left reverse punch

  • Shuffle forward and double inside chop strike, Kiai

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot back to a left forward stance with a left outside chop strike, right inside strike with left cover

  • Step right to a right forward stance with a right upward block and a left reverse stab

  • Slide right back to a left forward stance with a left downward block, shuffle forward with a right throw, Kiai
  • Slide right hand down sai, retrieve 3rd sai (pretend) from waistband, slide right foot to neutral stance

Yunbi stance, slide right foot out and flip sais out to 2nd position, scan, return to 1st position and slide right in to yunbi stance, bow
