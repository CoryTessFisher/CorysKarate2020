Escrima II (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Present. Bow. Present. Face partner. Bow. Present.

This is a partner form.

Defender starts on step 1.
Attacker starts by stepping forward with right foot to a right fighting stance with a right cob (inside strike). Continue with step 4.

  1. Slide left foot back to right fighting stance, right cob (inside block), slide right foot back to a left fighting stance, left cob (inside block)
  • Slide left foot back to right fighting stance, right X (Inside block, outside block)
  • Slide right foot back to left fighting stance, left X (Inside block, outside block)
  • Slide left foot back to right fighting stance, right X (Inside block, outside block)
  1. Spinning ordabess (see below)
  • Tuck right escrima under left arm, left escrima circles high for a left outward block
  • Right downward backhand block
  • Step back with right to a left fighting stance, left outward block
  1. Rodondo 3 / Heaven 3 (see below)
  • Step back left to a right fighting stance (one step for all 3 moves)
  • Tuck left escrima under right arm, right inside block, left outside block, right escrima circles high for a right inside block
  1. Slide left foot forward to left fighting stance, left cob (inside strike)
  • Slide right foot forward to right fighting stance, right X (Inside strike, outside strike)
  • Slide left foot forward to left fighting stance, left X (Inside strike, outside strike)
  • Slide right foot forward to right fighting stance, right X (Inside strike, outside strike)
  1. Spinning ordabess (see below)
  • Tuck right escrima under left arm, left escrima circles high for an left inside strike
  • Step behind with left foot, right downward backhand strike
  • Spin around to a left fighting stance, left outward strike
  1. Rodondo 3 / Heaven 3 (see below)
  • Step forward right to a right fighting stance (one step for all 3 moves)
  • Tuck left escrima under right arm, right inside strike, left outside strike, right escrima circles high for a right inside strike

Repeat steps 1 - 6. This is done by using the last right inside strike as the first cob strike on step 1.
