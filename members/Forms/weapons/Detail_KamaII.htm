Kama II (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start at center back of ring in a right forward stance facing back with right kama extended (position 1) and left kama in ready hand (position 2)

. . . or . . .

Continue from end of Kama I

(90° counterclockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), left horizontal punch (right ready hand), rotate to a left forward stance with a right inside seven strike (left ready hand)

  2. Step right to a neutral stance with a simultaneous left punch (right ready hand), continue to a right forward stance with a right punch (left ready hand)

  • Left inside hammerfist strike, left outside hammerfist strike (right ready hand)
  1. Step left to a neutral stance with a simultaneous right punch (left ready hand), continue to a left forward stance with a left punch (right remains extended)
  • Horizontal scissor strike, Kiai

(270° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F) with fighting hands
  • Right turning spin kick, right sliding side kick, right kima stance with a right outside chop strike (left ready hand)
  • Pivot with a left reverse punch (right ready hand), right kima stance with a right inside elbow strike and right outside elbow strike (left ready hand), pivot with a left reverse punch (right ready hand)

(90° counterclockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), step left to a left forward stance with a right double downward diagonal strike

  2. Step right to a right forward stance with a left double downward diagonal strike
  1. Step left to a left forward stance with a left kama flip (to position 1) and end in a left downward hook strike (right ready hand)
  • Right kama flip (return to position 1) and end in a right downward hook strike (left remains extended)
  • Right knee strike, Kiai

(90° counterclockwise to B)

  • Look (B), set down in left kima stance (with fighting hands)

  1. Left upward hook strike (right ready hand)

  2. Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right inside seven strike (left ready hand)
  • Right front thrust kick, set down in a right forward stance with a left punch

  1. Slide right to a right kima stance while performing a right kama flip (to position 2 ready hand) with a right inside rake, left hammerfist strike

(180° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), slide left foot to a left forward stance with a simultaneous left overhead kama flip (to position 2) and left outside hammerfist strike (right ready hand), Kiai

Slide left foot back to right foot with crossed kamas, bow
