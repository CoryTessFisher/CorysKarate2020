Escrima I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Present. Bow. Present. Set.

This form demonstrates the basic escrima strikes in a set pattern. Guard with left.


(All moves to F)

  1. Step forward with left foot with a right thrust

  2. Step to right diagonal with right foot with a high left horizontal strike (dodge)

  3. Step to left diagonal with left foot with a high right horizontal strike (dodge)

  4. Step to right diagonal with right foot with a downard left diagonal strike

  5. Step to left diagonal with left foot with a downward right diagonal strike

  6. Step to right diagonal with right foot with a mid left horizontal strike (dodge)

  7. Step to left diagonal with left foot with a mid right horizontal strike (dodge)

  8. No step. Upward left diagonal strike

  9. Step right to right diagonal with an upward right diagonal strike

  10. Down on left knee with a low left horizontal strike

  11. Low right horizontal strike

  12. Stand with a upward vertical strike

  13. Downward vertical strike

  14. Step directly to the left into a left forward stance with a downward thrust, Kiai


Present. Bow. Present.
