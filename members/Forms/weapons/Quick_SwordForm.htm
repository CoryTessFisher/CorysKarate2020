Sword Form
Based on a traditional sword form by Kalman Csoka (4th Degree Black Belt & #1 ranked in Traditional Sword on N.A.S.K.A. Circuit)

Walk forward, present weapon, bow, kneel, sit, lay sword, bow, attach sword

Section 1:

Look front, step left, right horizontal knife
Look right, right chop strike, look front, hand to tsuba, stand, kashira strike, Kiai

Pre-step left, angled left forward stance, retract sword
Right angled forward stance, right upward diagonal cut
Grab right, angled left forward stance, left downward diagonal cut
Parry up with right foot in, right forward stance, right downward diagonal cut, Kiai

Left to right, sword in front, sword up, left back stance

Section 2:

Right to left, angled left forward stance, left downward diagonal cut
Angled right forward stance, right downward diagonal cut
Angled left forward stance, left upward diagonal cut
Angled right forward stance, right upward diagonal cut
Ready hand left, roll sword, left knife hand, slide left, parry up
Grab left, right side kima, left downward diagonal cut, Kiai

Side neutral stance, chiburi, noto

Section 3:

Look left, reverse grip draw, right side kima stance, left downward diagonal cut
Left chamber, pre-step back with left
Look right, figure 8, left forward stance, finish figure 8, brace sword, high block
Push blade, right side kima, left downward diagonal cut, Kiai

Section 4:

Look forward diagonal, regular grip
Scissor stance, slow right downward cut
Neutral stance, sword up
Right forward stance, vertical upward cut

Look front, C-step/chamber, right forward stance, straight thrust

Pull out, spin, look front diagonal, right forward stance, downward vertical cut
Side left forward stance, left upward parry, right forward stance, right downward diagonal cut
Right chamber, roll blade/unwind, right knee, straight thrust, pull out right side kima stance

Section 5:

Look back/diagonal, left forward stance, point sword
Scissor stance, sword up/vertical, left neutral stance, kashira strike, pull back
Left forward stance, left downward diagonal cut
Side right forward stance, right downward diagonal cut

360 Degree move: chamber, right neutral, left horizontal cut, spin, sword @ back, right knee, vertical downward cut, Kiai

Pre-step right, spin, look front, diagonal right forward stance, right downward diagonal cut

360 Degree move: chamber, left horizontal cut, cross sword, double jump, right side kima stance with a left downward digonal cut, Kiai

Pop to side left forward stance, chiburi, noto

Section 6:

Reverse grip draw, horizontal strike, chamber and grab left

360 Degree move: right neutral, left downward diagonal cut, spin, chamber, diagonal left forward stance, left downward diagonal cut

Swap left hand, roll sword, horizontal strike, diagonal right forward stance
Roll sword, chamber, swap left hand

360 Degree move: right neutral stance, horizontal strike, spin, swap right hand, diagonal left forward stance, high parry

Chamber, right downward diagonal cut, diagonal right forward stance
Figure 8, chamber
Exaggerated right front stance, left downward diagonal strike, Kiai

Right side neutral stance, chiburi, noto

Slide right foot back to left and face F, chamber right hand

Step out right, yunbi (right hand only)
