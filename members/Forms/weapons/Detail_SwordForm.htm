Sword Form (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Sword Form
Based on a traditional sword form by Kalman Csoka (4th Degree Black Belt & #1 ranked in Traditional Sword on N.A.S.K.A. Circuit)

Saya (scabbard)
Tsuba (guard)
Kashira (pommel)
Sageo (hanging cord)
Hakama (sword pants)
Obi (hakama belt)
Chiburi (remove blood from blade)
Noto (sheath sword)

Walk forward with sword sheathed in left hand.
Present weapon, bow over extended weapon.
Kneel, left, right. Sit on heels. Lay sword with blade towards yourself. Sitting bow (hands on floor).
Attach sword. Sitting bow. Hands on knees, elbows out.

Section 1:

F

  1. Looking F, step up with left foot, right knife hand horizontal at chest, left hand on saya.

  • Look R, right chop strike.

  1. Bring right hand around to F (with eyes following), right hand to tsuba.

  2. Stand with right foot up to left with knees bent, extend sword for a kashira strike, Kiai.

  • Pre-step out to side with left foot, turn hips to angled left forward stance, slowly retract sword.

  1. Rotate saya outward with left hand (do not rotate right hand)

  • Regular grip draw, right upward diagonal cut with pivot into angled right forward stance

  1. Tip to ceiling, grab with right hand, chamber behind head

  • Left downward diagonal cut with pivot into angled left forward stance

  • Parry up at left and slide right foot in (shoulder width) on ball of foot

  1. Tip to right, chamber at left shoulder

  • Step forward right to right forward stance with a right downward diagonal cut, Kiai

  1. Slide left foot to right with knees bent, sword in front (kashira at mid-waist with tip down 45)

  2. Sword up to ceiling (straight right arm) on right

  • Step back right to left back stance and drop sword even with right shoulder (elbows up)

Section 2:

F

  1. Looking F, slide right foot to left, chamber at right shoulder

  • Pre-step out to side with left foot, turn hips to angled left forward stance with a left downward diagonal cut

  1. Tip around to chamber at left shoulder, pivot to angled right forward stance with a right downward diagonal cut

  2. Tip to ceiling, chamber at right hip, pivot to angled left forward stance with a left upward diagonal cut

  3. Tip to ceiling, chamber at left hip (hands cross), pivot to angled right forward stance with a right upward diagonal cut

  4. Left ready hand, roll sword to left side (over head, right palm up and sword horizontal to left side, right palm down and sword over), left knife hand under blade

  • Slide left foot in (shoulder width) on ball of foot, blade parry up at right with left knife hand brace at wrist (blade travels through body, right elbow back)

  1. Tip around to left, grab left, chamber at right shoulder

  • Step forward right to a right side kima stance with a left downward diagonal cut, Kiai

  1. Slide left foot forward to shoulder width side neutral stance with a chiburi (sword up around head, left hand to saya, right downward snap)

  1. Noto (tip up to ceiling, tip down, across body, tip up, left thumb and index finger on sword, slide sword out to tip, saya out to sheath, sheathed sword in to obi)

Section 3:

L

  1. Look L, reverse grip draw (flip right to palm up as left hand rotates saya out)

  • Draw out and across, blade down in front (palm up), blade up to right shoulder (palm down) and grab with left

  1. Step out right to a right side kima stance with a left downward diagonal cut

  2. Left chamber, pre-step back with left foot (towards F)

R

  • Look R, figure 8, kashira leads to back right side (palm up), roll sword at right (palm up)

  • Pivot to left forward stance facing R while bringing sword to horizontal in front (palm down)

  • Finish figure 8 roll (palm up) and extend left hand to brace sword (left palm on back of sword)

  • Pull in and push up for a high block

  1. Push blade down and to right with left hand, bring up and chamber at right shoulder

  • Step out right to a right side kima stance with a left downward diagonal cut, Kiai

Section 4:

F/L

  1. Look F/L, flip right hand to regular grip

  • Step left foot behind right to scissor stance with a slow dramatic right downward cut (tip up to right, around head, cut)

  1. Step left foot forward to neutral stance and sword tip up, sword tip down (chamber at right hip) and around to a vertical upward cut with a right step to a right forward stance

F

  1. Look F, slide right foot to left (starting C-step), chamber hands straight down to ribs

  • Right food out to a right forward stance with a straight thrust (shoulder level, right arm fully extended)

F/R

  1. Pull out (shoulder level), slide right foot to left turning counterclockwise (facing B)

  • Sword up and over head to down back with a small right step to B

  • Continue turn to face F/R, step back with left to right forward stance with a downward vertical cut

  1. Step left foot to F in a left forward stance with hips facing F (still looking F/R)

  • Sword tip around to left, over head around to right, left upward parry block (don't straighten right)

  • Slight step with right to a right forward stance with a right downward diagonal cut

  1. Sword tip up and chamber at right shoulder (left hand out)

  • Roll blade tip up to ceiling, cross right forearm over left wrist and tip to floor at left side, tip up at left side of body at shoulder level, shift to left front bo stance (to side still looking F/R) and unwind hands

  • Right knee up to F/L (crane stance) with a straight thrust (shoulder level, right arm fully extended), Kiai

  • Pull out at shoulder height and set down in a right side kima stance

Section 5:

B/L

  1. Look B/L, step left to a left forward stance, flip sword to point B/L (up to ceiling and over)

  2. Step right foot in front of left to a scissor stance, sword tip up to ceiling (right arm extended),

  • Sit down into scissor stance with sword down to vertical at shoulder height (elbows out)

  1. Step forward with left to a left neutral stance and drop sword tip back (parallel to floor), fast strike with kashira to face, pull back to hip

  2. Straight up to chamber at right shoulder, step left to left forward stance with a left downward diagonal cut

  3. Tip around head to chamber at left shoulder, pivot to a side right forward bo stance with a right downward diagonal cut

  4. 360 Degree move:

  • (facing B/L) Sword over head to chamber at right shoulder, step right to right neutral stance with a left horizontal cut at shoulder height

  • (facing F/R) Continue motion over head to roll with a counterclockwise spin, drop sword down back

  • (facing B/L) Step forward with left to finish spin

  • Set down on right knee with a vertical downward cut, Kiai

F

  1. Pre-step back with right foot to F/R

  • Look F, turn clockwise to a right forward stance to F/R, swing sword over head to chamber at left shoulder, right downward diagonal cut

  1. 360 Degree move:

  • Sword tip around over head to chamber at right shoulder, step through right foot with a left horizontal cut at shoulder height

  • Continue motion with sword to roll over and cross right forearm over left wrist while jumping off both feet 360 degrees spin

  • Land in a right side kima stance with a left downward diagonal cut, Kiai

  1. Start chiburi (sword up around head)

  • Finish chiburi with a popping simultaneous left foot forward to a left side forward stance (left hand to saya, right downward snap)

  1. Noto (tip up to ceiling, tip down, across body, tip up, left thumb and index finger on sword, slide sword out to tip, saya out to sheath, sheathed sword in to obi)

Section 6:

F

  1. Reverse grip draw (flip right to palm up as left hand rotates saya out)

  • Draw out and across in front at head level (palm up), continue across to drop at right side and pivot to a right forward stance (palm up), blade up to chamber at right shoulder (palm down) and grab with left

  1. 360 Degree move:

  • Step forward with right foot to a right neutral stance with a left downward diagonal cut

  • Pre-step left behind and spin counterclockwise, bring sword straight up to chamber at right shoulder

  • Continue motion with left foot to a diagonal left forward stance to F/L with a left downward diagonal cut

  1. Swap left hand (from palm down to palm up), roll sword (tip down and tuck back / left forearm over right)

  • Slice across from left shoulder to right shoulder and shift to a diagonal right forward stance

  1. Roll sword back (tip back / right palm down) to chamber at right and swap left hand (to palm down)

  • 360 Degree move:

  • Step right to right neutral stance and slice across horizontally from right to left at shoulder height

  • Pre-step left behind and spin counterclockwise, swap right hand (roll left palm up and swap right grip to normal grip palm down)

  • Finish by swinging sword overhead and around to a high parry block at left with a diagonal left forward stance

  1. Lower sword to chamber at left

  • Right downward diagonal cut and shift into a diagonal right forward stance

  1. Figure 8 roll to a chamber at right shoulder

  • (Roll sword tip up to ceiling with left chamber, tip back and down to the floor, slide left foot in to neutral stance / tip up and to left side of body, tip back and down to the floor, tip up and to right, chamber at right shoulder and grab left)

  1. Step forward right into an exaggerated right front stance with a left downward diagonal strike (Lean right shoulder in and look down slightly) Kiai

  2. Slide left foot forward to shoulder width right side neutral stance with a chiburi (sword up around head, left hand to saya, right downward snap)

  3. Noto (tip up to ceiling, tip down, across body, tip up, left thumb and index finger on sword, slide sword out to tip, saya out to sheath, sheathed sword in to obi)

Slide right foot back to left and face F, chamber right hand

Step out right, yunbi (right hand only)
