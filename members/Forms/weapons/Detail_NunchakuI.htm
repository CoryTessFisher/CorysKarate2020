Nunchaku Part I (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Yunbi stance, bow, yunbi stance

(90° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), nunchaku to right shoulder, slide right foot to right forward stance with a left downward strike (Left fist at waist)

  2. Nunchaku to left shoulder, right downward strike, horizontal strike, nunchaku to left shoulder, transfer nunchaku under arm to right hand

  3. Nunchaku to right shoulder, step right to right forward stance with a left downward strike, horizontal strike, nunchaku to right shoulder, transfer nunchaku under arm to left hand

  4. Nunchaku to left shoulder, step left to left forward stance with a right downward strike, horizontal strike, nunchaku to left shoulder, transfer nunchaku under arm to right hand

  5. Nunchaku to right shoulder, nunchaku to left waist

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), step back with right foot and turn to a right forward stance, right overhead downward diagonal strike
  2. Nunchaku to right shoulder, left downward strike, horizontal strike, nunchaku to right shoulder, transfer nunchaku under arm to left hand
  3. Nunchaku to left shoulder, step left to left forward stance with a right downward strike, horizontal strike, nunchaku to left shoulder, transfer nunchaku under arm to right hand
  4. Nunchaku to right shoulder, step right to right forward stance with a left downward strike, horizontal strike, nunchaku to right shoulder, transfer nunchaku under arm to left hand
  5. Nunchaku to left shoulder, nunchaku to right waist

(90° counterclockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), pivot to left forward stance with a thrusting downward block, thrusting upward block
  2. Step right to a right forward stance with a double thrusting strike
  3. Nunchaku in left hand to left shoulder, step left to left forward stance with a right downward strike
  4. Nunchaku to left waist and behind back, swap to right hand, right downward figure eight, clutch, step right to right forward stance with a right thrusting strike, Kiai
