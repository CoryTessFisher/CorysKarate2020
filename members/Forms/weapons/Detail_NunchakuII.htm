Nunchaku Part II (Detailed)

(F/L)     Front (F)     (F/R)
(L) Left              Right (R)
(B/L)      Back (B)     (B/R)

 

Start at center of ring in a right forward stance facing right nunchaku extended (in a punch) and left nunchaku at waist

. . . or . . .

Continue from end of Nunchaku I

(90° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot forward to a right forward stance, with a right downward block and remove left nunchaku from waist

  • Step left to a left forward stance with a double poke strike

(180° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), slide right foot to a right forward stance with a right butt strike, left butt strike

(90° counterclockwise to B)

  1. Look (B), rotate to a left forward stance with a left hammerfist strike and a right punch

  • Left jump front kick, left step side kick, left kima stance, left backfist strike, left upward hammerfist strike, Kiai

(90° clockwise to L)

  1. Look (L), (nunchaku to left shoulder) slide right foot to a right forward stance with a left double downward diagonal strike

  • (Nunchaku to right shoulder) slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right double downward diagonal strike

  • (Nunchaku to left shoulder) slide right foot to a right forward stance with a left double downward diagonal strike
  1. Slide left foot to a kima stance with right nunchaku to right shoulder and left nunchaku to left waist
  • Alternating spin, alternating spin, alternating spin (alternative option is alternating figure 8s)
  • Single spin right nunchaku to right shoulder
  • Double figure 8, clutch

(180° clockwise to R)

  1. Look (R), slide right foot back to a right forward stance with an left double overhead downward diagonal strike

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right double vertical spin

  • Slide right foot to a right forward stance with a left double vertical spin

  • Slide left foot to a left forward stance with a right double vertical spin

  • Double crossing figure 8, double crossing figure 8, double crossing figure 8, clutch (alternative option is double crossing figure 8s with an extra spin)

(270° clockwise to F)

  1. Look (F), right turn kick (set down in right forward stance), left front thrust kick, left punch, right reverse punch

  • (Slide right foot forward and right hand back) right scissor sweep, go down on left knee with a double downward strike, Kiai

Stand and left foot to right, yunbi stance, bow, yunbi stance
