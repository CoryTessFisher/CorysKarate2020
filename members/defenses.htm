Defenses

BREATHING EASY (video)
Attack: Left hand throat grab
Defense:
1) Elbow down to break hold.
2) Right snapping palm heel strike to face.
3) Left knee to opponent’s groin.
4) Set down left leg, pivot, left elbow, Kiai.
5) Left fighting stance.

SLAP STICK (video)
Attack: Right hand slap to face
Defense:
1) Left arm shunt block, in neutral stance.
2) Step forward, right palm heel to ribs.
3) Left inside hammer fist strike to jaw.
4) Right thrusting palm heel to face, Kiai .
5) Shift to right fighting stance.

MACE OF AGGRESSION (video)
Attack: 2 hand lapel grab
Defense:
1) Reach across both arm with left.
2) Step in right, right inside hammerfist to head.
3) Downward hammerfist - forearm strike down.
4) Right inside elbow (temple/jaw), right outward elbow (nose), Kiai.
5) Right fighting stance.

UPROOTING WINGS (video)

3) Right arm right shoulder grab crossing body.
4) Right knee strike, right knee strike, Kiai.
5) Left fighting stance.

GRASP OF DEATH (video)
Attack: Headlock bent over - opponent on left

2) Switch stance by sliding right back & left around front.
3) Left arm bar (left blade of forearm down, right wrist up - tight to body).
4) Left hand grab hair, right knee to face, Kiai.
5) Cover out.

4) Ax kickd, Kiai.
5) Cover out.
