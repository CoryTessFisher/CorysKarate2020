Kicks
Why do we learn so many kicks?

There are many good reasons. Some of these include:

  • The superior strength of our legs
  • How far we can kick (as opposed to how far we can strike)
  • Making use of every weapon available to us
  • Athleticism (becoming physically fit)
  • This is karate... duh!

Learning kicks can be challenging. Doing them well will require a dedicated effort on your part. Make that effort to excel at your kicks and you will be a much more effective martial artist. Just watching others performs these kicks will not be enough - you must actually come to class & practice!
