Classes

Class Availability
Classes are based upon both the age and rank of the students. We have classes appropriate for students age 5 through adult. We have classes for all belt ranks and encourage students to continue to train with us through advanced black belt degrees.

We are currently accepting new student requests for our waiting list.

Please email Cory Fisher at or use our contact form if you have any specific questions and to check on class availability.


Current Schedule
Beginning students 7 years old and younger attend a Junior Dragons class twice weekly. Due to the young age of the students they follow a modified belt rank system. Beginning and intermediate students 8 years old and above attend 2-3 times a week. These students follow the traditional Tan Ren Do Karate belt rank system.

In addition, motivated intermediate and advanced students are encouraged to join the Black Belt Club. This entitles the students to additional classes and teaching opportunities.

Classes are scheduled based upon what is best for the students. Class composition is based upon both the age and rank of the students. View the schedule to see the current class times for specific ages and ranks.


Monthly Calendar
Classes at Cory's Karate are held in Springville, Utah on Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday. The subject matter varies daily and is specified on a monthly calendar. View the calendar to see what we will be working on today. Remember to come prepared!
